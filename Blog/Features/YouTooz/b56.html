Banjo/Conker Spotted!

YouTooz Incoming! ~ Dec 10, 2024
Banjo-Kazooie & Conker YouTooz

Nine Inch Nostalgia

Banjo-Kazooie and Conker 9" plushies have been added to the YouTooz website, with a preorder date of December 17th, 2024, and a release window of May 1st to May 31st, 2025! Each one will cost $29.99, can be shipped worldwide, and features Rare's instantly recognisable characters in iconic poses!

YooTooz is a collectibles site that specialises in limited-time drops of officially licensed toys since 2019. They have a penchant for bringing memorable pop-culture merchandise to your homes, featuring heroes and role models of the internet, memes, and video game IPs.

Their products span over 400 pieces now, and although each piece is only available for a limited time, they give you plenty of opportunity to grab the bits you want before they are discontinued.

If you thrive on FOMO, then this is a site you should bookmark, and even if you don't, then you better get a wiggle on because the upcoming Banjo-Kazooie and Conker plushies are incredibly cute and bring back nostalgia in spades for fans of the games!

As a bonus, YouTooz are also releasing a 6.3" Banjo-Kazooie "Banjo-Ka-Snoozie" nightlight, which will release between April 1st and April 30th 2025, also for $29.99!

Plushie Perfection

Each toy is made from minky, tricot, and felt materials, stuffed with 100% PP cotton, and features official licensing from Rare, which means you're getting the best quality available and with a likeness you can trust.

The Conker plushie has particularly stolen the show with his trademark helmet, cigar, and dual wielding uzis! The Banjo-Kazooie ones feature intricate detailing too, with Banjo's backpack and necklace, and Kazooie looking super cutesy and ready to adventure.

With the SKUs labelled as "All Out Battle" "Tuckered Out" and "Besties", it leaves the door open to future iterations of the lovable characters in new poses and costumes. Conker has a massive wardrobe, so he could be dressed as anything, like Neo from the Matrix or maybe even riding a T-Rex!

YouTooz last endeavoured into N64 territory with 4.8" Conker's Bad Fur Day statues, mugs, and pins, as well as a slew of Banjo-Kazooie figurines and merch in 2022, and they even went as far as to tease us with a Super Mario 64 BLJ (backwards Long Jump) meme concept back in 2021, though the odds of that ever getting OK'd by Nintendo were slim to none.
