Commissioned Characters

Every once in a while something pops up in my feed that make me say, "Whoa, that's seriously cool!". As a result of having connections to various N64-related accounts, and seeing their shared posts, I stumbled upon Emily Victorious and her fantastic Rare-inspired creations!

Through a combination of 3D printing and model making artistry, Emily has faithfully recreated a plethora of googly-eyed Rare-recognisable characters, such as T.T. from DKR, Grunty, Leaky, Cheato, Loggo, Juju, Flotsam, Clanker, as well as various Banjo-Kazooie/Tooie transformations, including termite, croc, walrus, pumpkin, washing machine, bee, stoney, detonator, snowball, submarine, van T-Rex and even Dragon-Kazooie!

There are a huge variety to choose from, but my hands-down absolute personal favourites are T.T., the stopwatch character from Diddy Kong Racing, and the iconic and charming N64 logo transformation! All of these are available to purchase as commissions on her store!
