N64brew Game Jam No. 6

Compete for Prizes! ~ Dec 1, 2025

Homebrew Heroes

Following last year's epic Game Jam #5 and the three incredible winning entries, N64brew are now preparing for their 6th annual event, which sees individuals, or teams, go head-to-head crafting the latest and greatest mini-games for the Nintendo 64 using the Libdragon SDK, all in the name of raising a tonne of cash for charity!

Welcome to the second N64brew Game Jam!

Compete against other players and teams to make an N64 game and help out a charity! The competition starts December 1st, the theme will be announced December 8th, and the competition ends February 1st.

Donation Drive

The prize pool has been started off at $100 and will hopefully be added to via generous donors throughout the course of the competition. If you would like to donate, then please use PayPal and send your donations directly to u64brew@gmail.com using the "Friends and Family" option. As ever, the winners will be able to donate the final sum of money to the charity of their choosing when the competition ends after February 1st, 2026.

I will be keeping my beady eye on the upcoming details when they are announced on December the 8th, and I greatly look forward to seeing some fresh and exciting homebrew emerging in the coming months.
