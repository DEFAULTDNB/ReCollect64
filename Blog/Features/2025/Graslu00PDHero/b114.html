Graslu00 Beats Perfect Dark

WITH A GUITAR! ~ Apr 10, 2025
Accoustic Dark

Stringing us Along

Graslu00 has now decimated Perfect Dark with a Guitar Hero controller, adding to previous articles about him smashing GoldenEye with both a Guitar Hero controller and an InterAct V3 steering wheel.

Within his latest five-and-a-quarter-hour stream, the speedrunner hammers not only the single-player mode on Perfect Agent difficulty, but he also mops up all the cheats and takes on 2 Dark Sims on challenge 29, which, for those who don't know, is expert-level difficulty, even with a regular controller!

Switching the controller style to 1.2 (One Handed), turning off look-ahead and reverse pitch, he is able to navigate the entire game with his trademark cool-as-a-cucumber demeanour. Check it out below:

Don't Fret

With this recent spate of off-the-wall speed runs, Graslu00 is set to attack GoldenEye with an UltraRacer 64 and no doubt will also be tackling Perfect Dark with the V3 wheel and the UltraRacer 64 in due course!

I wonder what will be next for the plucky speedrunner. Regardless, rest assured that ReCollect64 will be watching and covering any future wacky follow-ups!
