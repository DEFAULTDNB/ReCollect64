Marios Modules Released!

Old Plumber, New Tricks ~ Feb 8, 2025

Smooth Moves

Rovertronic, of Mario in the Multiverse fame, has today released his latest project; Mario's Modules!

The 1.1.0 patch incorporates the latest version of the Super Mario 64 ROM hack, which sees Mario becoming a modifiable cyborg of sorts, with heaps of special abilities at his command once you've found them.

 

Mario is an ancient automaton that must explore the manufacturing dungeon and collect modules to craft moves from scratch.

The soundtrack used in the hack is from an Undertale fangame called SwApFell: I Hate You., by Igorsantusa and Calem28.

Rovertronic also released an explanitory video where you can see the variety and multitude of combinations possible. Take a look here:

Jack of all Trades

The idea behind this ROM hack is brilliantly clever. Yu have full control over the plumbers moveset and as such, you can craft your favourite version of him.

The premise is simple: discover moves, collect coins, use coins to open chests, gain better skills, and grab keys and stars! The layout of the game is also incredibly intuitive, with a toolbox at your disposal by pressing the start button, which is full of modules that you discover around the dungeon. There appear to be 24 slots for abilities currently.

Modifiers such as detecting a button press, if Mario touches the ground, or adding a delay can all be strung together in eight strong combination chains. With the expansive range of moves and modifiers, the possibilities are endless. Some basic examples include giving Mario a jump with a kick at the same time, giving him double height jump, activate a wall jump upon pressing a button, or adding a hover ability immediately after a jump. Bringing all of these together, you really have to use your brain to craft the moves that you require to beat the task at hand. It's truly genius!

This ROM hack works perfectly on Rosalina's Mupen GUI but is also playable on real hardware too thanks to the latest 1.1.0 revision of the Mario's Modules patch. All you need is the Super Mario 64 (U) (V1.0) [!].z64 ROM, with an MD5 of 20B854B239203BAF6C961B850A4A51A2, the patch and ROM patching site, both of which are linked below. The resulting ROM will have an MD5 of 011C08DDA3BCDBFF0E569AEA04971A3C, and you're ready to explore!
