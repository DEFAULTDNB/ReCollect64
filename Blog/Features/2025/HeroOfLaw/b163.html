Hero of Law Released

An Ace Attorney OOT Mod! ~ Jun 29, 2025

OBJECTION!!

Hero of Law is a complete overhaul mod that's an amalgamation of the judicial action of the Ace Attorney games set in the land of Hyrule with a cast of familiar Zelda characters.

Set primarily in the Hyrule Grand Court, this courtroom drama simulator does a fantastic job of portraying the stressful job role and internal monologues from Link, this game's professional lawyer of seven years!

You have quick access to dialogue history as well as the court records that contain your credentials and evidence, and you can interrogate, prosecute and defend clients against adversaries such as Dragmire (yes, Ganondorf Dragmire) along with cameos from homicide detective Impa of Sheika Force, all to some wonderfully remixed music from the OOT score!

This game is a masterclass in polish, with beautifully scripted dialogue, cut scenes and dramatic exchanges. The whole thing looks gorgeous and runs beautifully too.

In Session

To play the game, you can patch the ROM on the site itself using Shygoo's Web Patcher & Swapper. All you need to provide is the base USA Ocarina of Time *.Z64 ROM with the MD5 5BD1FE107BF8106B2AB6650ABECD54D6, then let the site do the rest. Alternatively, you can download the xDelta patches and use any other method you wish.

The resulting 8 MB ROM, with the MD5 847C2D82C7CA721E873545486F0BC780, runs perfectly on all emulators I tested and even on flashcarts on real hardware. Keep an eye out for my review soon!
