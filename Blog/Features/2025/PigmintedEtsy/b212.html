A couple of weeks ago I began following a user on Twitter known as Pigminted purely because the woodwork projects they undertake are nothing short of inspiring. What Pigminted does is take popular games and media and lovingly craft decorative wooden effigies of the cartridges, protective boxes based on the box art, slip covers for guidebooks and plenty more besides.

Currently stocking 17 different N64-based designs, there are options on some to include a Raspberry Pi Zero, which means that you can use this as a portable emulation device as well as a high quality show piece!
