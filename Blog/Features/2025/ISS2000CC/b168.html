ISS2K Cup Champions Released

Fantasy Football

Today a fresh footy-focused hack has arrived for avid soccer fans around the world. International Super Star Soccer 2000: Cup Champions is a modest hack that aims to pit cup champion players and teams from across the last 25 years against one another in the ultimate play-off.

Using the community tools by Nicola Cusano and Pedro Seidel, it's possible to craft countless modifications based on your specific tastes. This one is "Cup Champions" and as the name implies, the teams have all been tuned by Luis Raul Angrisano to be the best of the best within their cup winning eras.

 

Features:

1) All WC champions from 2002 to 2022

2) Portugal 2024

3) Copa América champions : Colombia 2001, Chile 2015-2016, Uruguay 2011

4) Venezuela Copa América 2011 (4th place)

5) Netherlands WC 2010 (2nd place)

6) Perú Copa América 2019 (2nd place)

With teams customised to precisely mirror their cup-winning counterparts, you could pit the 2001 Copa America winners, Colombia, against the 2024 World Cup winners, Portugal, for example, to play out fantasy matches in the comfort of your own home.

The hack is available from the International Superstar Soccer (Nintendo 64) Fans Facebook site and comes pre-patched for ease of use, though without a patch file to share, I cannot directly link it here, but it's very easy to find.
