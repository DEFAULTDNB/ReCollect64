Strumming & Strafing

In what may sound like an absolute fever dream, GoldenEye expert and friend of the site, Graslu00 has done something rather incredible: he's only gone and beaten GoldenEye 007 with nothing more than a Guitar Hero controller!

Within the three-hour stream, Graslu00 easily smashes the 00 Agent difficulty in under one hour fifty-four minutes and even has time to go back and unlock all of the available cheats!

You can take a look at the god-tier action in the VOD below!

Note-worthy Execution

It's worth pointing out that the layout of the guitar provides some interesting configuration. The strum bar becomes a rapid-fire trigger, the whammy bar is the start button, the D-pad is used to strafe, the fret buttons control movement, and in a stylish moment of ultimate-bad-assery, lifting the neck is mapped to reload. Using the 1.2 Solitaire layout, auto-aim on, and look ahead off, he makes it look incredibly easy throughout the entire 20-level onslaught.

Again, I state that this is being played on 00 Agent difficulty, and yet it looks like a walk in the park. Testament to his ability and skills within one of the best games on the system.
