F4F BK Duet Announced

Big Boy Bundle

Some eight months after the last Banjo-Kazooie/Tooie-related figurine release of Tooty, First 4 Figures are back with another banger of a collector's item; it's a Banjo-Kazooie Duet effigy available in three sizes up to 1/4 scale!

One has to ask, what does 1/4 scale mean when it comes to fictitious video game characters? Well, Banjo is unofficially estimated to be a humble 4' 8" tall, so this model should measure up to at least 30 cm tall. Typically, figurines are designed at 1/7th scale, so this version should be colossal, though the exact measurements are yet to be divulged.

The Definitive Duet

Retailing at a whopping $731.99 if you preorder today, or $773.99 thereafter, the definitive edition 1/4 scale Banjo-Kazooie Duet will pack the three characters, Banjo, Kazooie and Bottles, plus a little frog and two honeycomb props, all depicting the iconic opening screen of the game that now resides in the ownership of Microsoft and Rare.

Keep your eyes peeled for the anticipated 9th of December updates on the official First 4 Figures social sites.
