BK Halloween 2025 Announced

Banjo-Witch Project

With the inevitable end of summer 2025 comes autumn 2025, which ushers in chillier weather, trees shedding their leaves and an influx of the pumpkin-spiced trash they serve up at coffee places. It isn't all doom and gloom though, because Markus Kurko has announced today that Banjo-Kazooie Halloween 2025 is now in the works, and a playable version should be with us by the 31st of October!

Providing us just one tantalising BK-themed Blair Witch-esque image and a small paragraph of information, Kurko is wisely keeping his latest creation under wraps until the trick-or-treating day is upon us:

 

First of all, I want to thank everyone who participated in the poll, as well as everyone who supports me month after month. Without all of you, this wouldn't be possible, thanks so much!

According to the results of the polls published on Patreon, YouTube, and Instagram, the vast majority wants a Halloween Mod, so your wish is my command.

Development of the game has already begun, but I can't show anything yet. The idea is for the game to include two levels, with about 6/8 Jiggies each. As I mentioned before, the game will be less cartoonish and more horror-oriented, and it may even have touches of gore, so it will not be recommended for children under 16.

The Public Version will be on October 31st, and I'd like to have the Early Access ready by the 25th, however, I can't guarantee that 100% yet.

I'm going to have a short trailer ready with all the details by the end of September or early October.

That's all for now, I hope you're as excited as I am about this small project!!

Regards,

Mark Kurko
