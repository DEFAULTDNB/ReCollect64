Mario's Modules 2 Teaser

Rovertronic has been busy! ~ Dec 15, 2025

Serious Sequels

Rovertronic has today teased a sequel to his incredibly clever Mario ROM hack, Mario's Modules, which was released earlier this year. The aptly named Mario's Modules 2, sees the titular plumber's dungeon-crawling adventure bolstered by a plethora of updates, including an overhauled UI, modular enemies, in-game cosmetics, mystery chests, new modules and combos, plus procedurally generated dungeons and a stunning 60 FPS display mode!

Modular Moments

The things that stuck out in the trailer for me are the procedurally genrated dungeons and the if statements. With just these two modifiers alone, the possibilities for replayability are quite literally endless with a multitude of moves and infinite areas to explore.

Given Rovertronic's abilities within the realm of Super Mario 64 ROM hacking, I don't doubt that this will be a substantial and memorable update to the Mario's Modules series. As soon as Rovert announces more information and a release date, I will keep you all updated.
