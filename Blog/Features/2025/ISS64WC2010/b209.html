ISS64 World Cup 2010 Released

A Quick Friendly

Following on from his recent release of Ronaldinho Soccer PES 6 Rev B, Chris Jaroki is back with his latest creation, International Superstar Soccer 64 - World Cup 2010, which is best described as a season update patch for ISS64 to include the entire World Cup 2010 roster.

This time around, Chris states that he has put together an overhaul patch for ISS64 that clones all the relevant player information found in Pro Evolution Soccer 2011, which pertains to the South Africa 2010 World Cup. He also sadly announced that this will be his final ROM hack for ISS64.

Patch-Perfect

The good news is that Chris has freely distributed an IPS file, so I can link it here for you to patch your own ROM. The process is extremely easy to do and only takes a matter of seconds once you have the files.

All you need is the "International Superstar Soccer 64 (USA).z64" ROM, with an MD5 of 6A345402AE1DB5CE1041365E36126BCE, and the supplied IPS patch found over at the link below. Using a site such as Marc Robledo's JS ROM patcher, you should have a patched output file with a resulting MD5 of 7BB2C1BC8FF5B0f9CCA420851753409D.

This 8 MB "ISS64 PES2011"-headed ROM works perfectly on emulators as well as flash carts and brings you all the players, teams and statistics of the 2010 World Cup so that you can relive the event or rewrite your own version of it by toppling Spain before they can get their hands on the coveted trophy.
