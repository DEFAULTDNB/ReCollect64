Lego Clock Town Spotted!

Lego Locations

Following on from Super Brick Dude's last release, this week he has been sharing some preliminary work on his next feat of miniature architecture in the shape of Clock Town from The Legend of Zelda: Majora's Mask!

The project began with the brilliant builder collating various maps and artwork as reference material, and then a scaled base was assembled to lay out the town with the iconic tower timepiece at the centre, complete with a custom clock sticker on the front for maximum detailing! Check out the current progress below:

Passion Project

With some smaller details already coming into focus, we can make out the intricate central hub, the ramps and doors of the tower, and even the Treasure Chest shop with incredible clarity. The encapsulating walls wrap around Termina's town, and the teeny tiny trees, paths and waterways are so cleverly observed and authentically textured that I still cannot believe that Nintendo have not officially done this themselves, or, at very least, taken SuperBrickDude onboard and collaborated with his skills.

As an avid follower of all of SuperBrickDude's projects, I will be fastidiously refreshing my feed daily to see the latest progress with this project. Majora's Mask has always been my personal number one Zelda game, so I cannot wait to see how this one turns out and how much it will cost to build for ourselves when it eventually releases on Rebrickable.
