"Parallel Worlds"-Themed Hacks! ~ Sep 3, 2025
Hylian Modding Jam 2025

Ocarina of Sublime

Recently, five brand new ROM hacks themed around "parallel worlds" have become available for The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time thanks to the little-known Hylian Modding ROM hack Jam #1 2025, held on the Hylian Modding site.

The transformational hacks, which all work with the 1.00 version of the USA OOT ROM (MD5: 5BD1FE107BF8106B2AB6650ABECD54D6), include Barjack's Merda by Barjack, Halflight Shrine by PlushimaTitania, Reality Estate by Jinnosuke, Corrhaid and Chadlight, Return To Stone Tower by Nokaubur, and Mechanical Shrine by arthurc.

Barjack's Merda sees Link borrowing the mask of truth and adventuring outside around the town, exposing the shocking truths and exploring treatcherous mountains to seek your goal.

Halflight Shrine focuses on dungeons, bosses and puzzles to keep you entertained in a visually vibrant series of indoor environments. Playing as a Sheikah, you get to find and use the Halflight weaponry.

Mechanical Shrine is a dark and moody fortress full of puzzles and dangers. You must navigate your way through and defeat a boss to beat this ROM hack.

Reality Estate pits you against the monsters of Hyrule as an exterminator who must investigate a mansion for some perturbed owners. With modern scenery and objects, this hack is a little bit of a spooky mystery centred around a haunted house.

Finally, Return to Stone Tower flips your perspective and shifts gravity with some taxing puzzles and a decent boss fight at the end.
