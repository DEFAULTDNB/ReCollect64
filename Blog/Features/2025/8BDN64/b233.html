New 8BitDo 64 Variant

Classic Colours

8BitDo has today announced an updated colourway for their 64 controller, which now allows you to choose from black, white and now a brand new classic grey variant with the iconic coloured buttons.

Having purchased the black version to match my preorder console back in July, following a need to re-preorder the item from the original order I made several months prior, I really do think that this version is something I might also have to purchase!

I previously covered how to improve the 64 controller's input, as well as a look at an unofficial mod that made the buttons rather stylish too.

Funtastic Future

As annoying as it is that this option wasn't available day one, I can see 8BitDo producing plenty more variants to fill the niche now that the Analogue 3D has begun shipping. After all, some people are going to want the full four-pad salvo to really flex their brand-spanking-new 4K FPGA machines and ready themselves for some multiplayer mayhem with friends.

I would love to see the full Funtastic series get an outing, with jungle green, ice blue, fire orange, watermelon red, grape purple and smoke grey leading the charge, with perhaps a gold or extreme green variant offered too!

For now, you can preorder the new grey version with coloured buttons today, ready for your Analogue 3D consoles to arrive this side of 2026, hopefully. Don't forget to update them before use to get the maximum out of them!
