Hexen has Returned!

Beyond Heretic

Following QuakeCon 2025, Raven Software and Nightdive Studios, in collaboration with ID Studios, have shadowdropped (read: immediately released) Heretic and Hexen (plus all versions, DLC and more!) for NSW/PSN/Xbox and PC. A physical release will follow for most major platforms via Limited Run.

Using a modified Doom engine, Raven Software's Hexen was a sequel to 1994's Heretic, a game that never made it to the N64, but fortunately, the sequel did. Bringing innovative features to the FPS genre, such as vertical aiming and an inventory system, Hexen was devisive with N64 fans back in 1997, averaging just 60% in reviews around the time.
