GE LTK Netplay Cup VI Announced

Bonding Session

2026 is already looking bright, thanks to some fresh announcements from some familiar faces. Following last year's action-packed GoldenEye 007 Licence to Kill Netplay Cup V held throughout February and March of 2025, GoldenEye 007 speedrunner and competition host TH126 has announced that a sixth tournament will commence in February 2026!

Merry Christmas! Happy to announce that work has begun on the 6th iteration of the GoldenEye Netplay LTK Cup multiplayer tournament! It all goes down again in February. More details and registration in the coming weeks pic.twitter.com/CDkrWXUCJY

A View to Several Kills

Last year's tournament was an absolute nail-biter, with all 22 players battling it out through qualifying group stages, then into a series of best-of-three and best-of-five matches, before meeting up as the best of the best, which would eventually determine the finalists.

The LTK Cup V provided us with over 1350 kills throughout the tournament, which ultimately led to Graslu00 triumphing in first place, TH126 coming second, Grim clutching third place, and EternallyAries taking fourth. Graslu00 famously considered retiring from these tournaments following his "three-peat" victory in three consecutive tournaments (III, IV and V) in order to focus on fresh endeavours.

With this achievement, I'm considering my retirement from multiplayer competitions... Even if I do that, I'd still play and maybe even join tournaments but with a different mindset. Focus on having fun rather than just winning.

I wanted three wins in a row and I got them.

Will he re-enter this year? Keep your eyes peeled for updates in the following weeks and for the start of the competition commencing sometime around the first week of February.
