Upcoming Amped Up Updates

3D Portraits! ~ Oct 22, 2025

Famous Faces

Following the release of Mario Kart 64 Amped Up V3 and its subsequent 0.1, 0.2, 0.3, and 0.4 patches, Litronom has today shown progress of his latest WIP development version, which swaps the original 2D gif-looking renders for fully animated 3D models when selecting your racer!

A sneak peak of the next Mario Kart 64 - Amped Up update.
The "Player Select" screen will properly reflect the "3D Racer" settings, to unify the design across the game.

Facial Recognition

The nostalgic low poly-looking models of the characters will be a superb addition to the Amped Up mod, which already allows you to toggle between in-game 3D karting characters or the original 2D rendered images, once again pushing the limits and demonstrating what is actually possible on N64 hardware with dedication, time and effort.

Litronom has famously not been using any form of decompilation to manufacture these modifications, instead opting for the purest forms of ROM hacking magic and manipulation to provide these stunning additions, such as extra racetracks, new modes, updated models, and fresh soundtracks for the now 29-year-old racing title.
