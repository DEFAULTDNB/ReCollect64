ReCollect64 Site Updates!

Slow & Steady

Since the end of October, I have been implementing a long-anticipated feature, which will expand the ReCollect64 Collectors app beyond the mere 1800+ variants it currently holds and aims to add hundreds of new items such as consoles, controllers, and accessories!

Unfortunately, this takes time, and as a result, my usual output has been a little hampered, with any available spare time I have going into the Collectors App updates. The good news is that I have progressed quite well and have implemented an adapted (read: temporary) framework that integrates console boxes (front and back), console hardware (top and bottom views), and additional items such as manuals, controller variants and their respective regional boxes, of which there are tonnes!

The goal is to catalogue any and all variants, beginning with OEM consoles and controllers, eventually expanding into OEM accessories, and even some of the more interesting third-party peripherals.

(These are some examples of the images I wish to use to show off the hardware variety. I will also include images of the underside of the console from each region too!)

(As well as the widely known USA boxes, there are AUS/JPN/EUR and additional variants to collate and display too. Each box will include the pad and manual too.)

Building Blocks

While I do have a reasonable amount of free time right now, it is consumed with personal things that draw me away from my trusty laptop and make the process slower, and the knock-on effect is slowing down my news and review writing.

I will endeavour to spin plates and get the current database's games media up to scratch as soon as possible; it currently stands at "A" - "H" being 99% done, and "I" & "J" are currently WIP. I plan to chip away at this as and when I need a break from scouring the net for decent images of consoles, fixing them, compressing them, and building the relevant databases.

The main takeaway from this post is: please bear with me, because I have reviews lined up that need proofing, Q&As in my inbox, and news articles to catch up with, but they're all coming.
