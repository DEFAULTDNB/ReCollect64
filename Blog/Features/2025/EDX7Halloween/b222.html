Ever Ready Drive

EverDrive for the Nintendo 64 has existed since around 2011, with the V1, V2, V2.5, V3, X5 and X7 versions succeeding its impressive lineage. I first picked up an ED64 V2.5 back in 2014, and my experience was incredible. Barring the select few games that had not been cracked yet, it ran everything I threw at it and provided a solid platform to play my entire library with ease. I have since upgraded to the superb X7 a couple of years ago because I wanted to take advantage of the RTC, save on reset, GameShark cheats, UltraCIC III, and faster loading times.
