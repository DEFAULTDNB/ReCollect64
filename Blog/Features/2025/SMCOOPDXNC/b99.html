SM64 Coop DX Updates

The Whole Gang

In July 2024, I reported on Super Mario 64 Coop Deluxe, a PC mod of Super Mario 64 that allows you to play the original game cooperatively, with a solid 60 FPS, a day-to-night cycle, character palete customisation, and much more besides.

This week, the SM64 Coop DX team has officially added a new character to their "Character Select Pack". The pack previously contained Yoshi, Birdo, Pauline, Peach, Daisy, Toadette and Spike. The latest addition now adds Rosalina!

Each character has their signature moves, so Yoshi has his flutter, Rosalina is shrouded with Lumas when she spin-jumps, and Birdo can fire an egg projectile that you can use as a moving platform.

The attention to detail is great, and each character has an immediately recognisable silhouette and colour scheme. Birdo is a particularly interesting retro addition, as the character first appeared in Super Mario Bros 2 (Doki Doki Panic) in 1988 as an enemy!

 

Extra Characters V8.1

  • Rosalina's Spin hitbox serves as a kick now.
  • Picking up items as Rosalina is easier now.
  • Infinite Jumps glitch has been fixed.
  • Yoshi can now flutter in Long Jump and Triple Jump.
  • Rosalina's Hand Cap and Wing Cap hand states have been fixed.
  • Spinning Attack model's player colors that affected local players have been fixed.
  • WIP in the mod's title has been removed which was left by mistake

Latest and Greatest

The newest version of SM64 Coop DX, 1.2.1, which was released on February the 15th 2025, comes in several variants, including Linux, Mac, Windows OpenGL, and Windows Direct X. There is also a development build of the Android edition for those who wish to try this on the move.

For the main builds, the team has managed to cram in widescreen support, uncapped FPS, and a staggering 16-player online multiplayer mode that makes for a fun-packed an hilariously chaotic experience.

To play the game, simply drag and drop the Super Mario 64 (U) *.Z64 ROM into the executable and enjoy. The latest character pack can be downloaded and chucked in the "Mods" folder, ready to use.

The main site also houses a huge number of resources such as gameplay modifiers, ROM hacks, and more custom character packs. Notably, there are over 300 characters to choose from, including Earth Worm Jim, Goemon, Slippy Toad, Oswald the Rabbit, Count Bleck, Carl Johnson, SpongeBob, Kirby, Charizard, and even Croc!
