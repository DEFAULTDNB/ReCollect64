64 Controllers: Now Shipping!

Elating Emails

This morning I received an email confirming the shipping status of the much-anticipated 8BitDo 64 Controller, which is built to work on Analogue's FPGA-based N64 console, which itself is set to land next month.

Finding this information in my inbox, I was surprised to see that the product has actually been dispatched from the depot and is winging its way to my home address as I type. It feels surreal to be experiencing this moment after waiting nearly nine months since its first announcement.

This is a great indicator that the Analogue 3D is indeed due for delivery at the end of August, and I for one certainly cannot wait to get my teeth stuck into reviewing the controller, the console, and of course, the games!

 

The 8BitDo 64 Bluetooth Controller revives the iconic N64 layout, but wrapped in an ergonomic, updated body. A precision hall-effect joystick with the original gate and signature concave rubber joystick cap. Say goodbye to the infamous loose joysticks. We’re bringing back the signature C-buttons, A/B and Start—with the button sizes, positioning and feel just like the original.

Multiplayer on the N64 defined an era, and now with Analogue 3D and the 8BitDo 64 Controllers, that era is redefined. The 8BitDo 64 Controller, designed hand-in-hand with Analogue, creates an experience like no other when paired with Analogue 3D. It’s a celebration of the N64, the greatest multiplayer system ever.

It’s Bluetooth-ready, with vibration support and platform compatibility with the new Analogue 3D, Switch, (including N64 Online) and more. Play N64 games like never before—with the best of both worlds in your hands.

The 64 controller is currently still available for purchase, so if you desire one, even for Mobile/PC gaming, check out Amazon US or Amazon EU depending on your location.
