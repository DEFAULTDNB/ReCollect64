Patchmakers Workshop Released

SM64 ROM Hack by CowQuack ~ May 29, 2025

Got it Sewn Up

Today, ROM hacker and modder CowQuack has released the much-anticipated Tim Burton-esque Super Mario 64 ROM hack, The Patchmaker's Workshop.

The hack brings years worth of modding work into one marvellously creepy creation that sees Mario team up with a new partner across several brand new worlds in a bid to thwart "the crazed creator of all that surrounds", the Patchmaker.

 

"One day, whilst crafting a plush doll, something miraculous occured. The doll smiled at me."

Mario awakes inside a charming workshop with a dark past, with Peach and Bowser transformed into plush dolls sitting beside him.

They had all been attacked by a man named The Patchmaker... To save them, he needs to team up with a creation of that man, the living doll Sewnia.

Embark across 12 different worlds, collecting a new ability in each that helps Mario traverse that area.

What secrets does this workshop hold? What goals does The Patchmaker have?

...And how did Sewnia come to life exactly?

The Patchmaker's Workshop features a fully original soundtrack composed by ornevelder, Tori Draws Stuff, and CowQuack.

Patch-tastic

The mod transforms the standard 8 MB ROM into a 50 MB monster, brimming with custom levels, music, characters, and an epic story and dialogue exclusive to this creation.

Mario has a multitude of interesting abilities, and even the HUD is given a makeover, with the power bar displayed in the top left corner as beads and a D-Pad at the bottom left with your abilities assigned to the various directions.

To play the ROM hack, all you need to do is patch the USA Super Mario 64 *.Z64 ROM (MD5: 20b854b239203baf6c961b850a4a51a2) with the BPS provided in the link below. The patched ROM will have an MD5 of 1ED9253297BA537B0C3F86824C59405B and is fully playable on consoles via flashcart or through emulators such as Rosalie's Mupen GUI or Ares.
