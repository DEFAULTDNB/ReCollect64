Graslu00 Beats Perfect Dark

WITH A WHEEL! ~ Apr 17, 2025
Perfect Park(ing)

All the Gear & Every Idea

Graslu00 continues his bizarre streak against N64 FPS games, this time battering Perfect Dark with a steering wheel. This means he has now beaten GoldenEye 007 with a Guitar Hero controller, an InterAct V3 steering wheel and then smashed Perfect Dark with a Guitar Hero controller too!

Using the InterAct V3 steering wheel controller, which I rated a solid 8 out of 10 in my third-ever review for ReCollect64, Graslu00 beats the single-player mode on Perfect difficulty (within 3 hrs 45 mins), unlocks all cheats and beats Challenge 29 in under five and a half hours; a feat that most of us couldn't best with an OEM controller!

Switching the controller style to 1.1 and turning off reverse pitch, he makes it look second nature ploughing through the game at full tilt with relative ease. Check it out below:

Skid-ar Warrior

Having now beaten both Perfect Dark and GoldenEye with a wheel and with a guitar, the next challenge could be anything. You never know; he may yet pull out a Tsurikon Fishing controller, a QuickShot NitroStick, and Arcade Shark or something even more obscure and wacky like the Densha De Go! train lever, or perhaps even the DDR Disney Dancing Museum dancemat.

He recently hinted that he would be using the UltraRacer 64 to blitz those games in a stream coming soon, so it's just a matter of time before Graslu00 does it again, and no doubt, I'll be glued to my screen watching every moment of it!
