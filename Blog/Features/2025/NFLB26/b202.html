NFL Blitz 2026 Released

Refreshed Lineups

Following the release of NFL Blitz Silver Special Edition, NFL Blitz 2026 has today been made public by the NFL Blitz Resurrection team!

The modding crew, comprised of @ScallyCap, @UltraMagnus1982, @averysempervive and @vaycant, have squeezed in as many last-minute roster changes and signings as they could before the release of the updated ROM. Much of that was covered here in last months roster reveal.

For anyone wondering, the difference between Silver Special Edition and 2026 is that this edition is fully current, whereas the Silver Special Edition incorporates all-time rosters from over the past 25 years.

Biannual Blitz

For the last few versions of NFL Blitz Resurrection, we have seen a mid-year "V2" update to keep the current rosters in line with their real-life counterparts. I've said it before, and I will say it again: getting regular modern updates for games made in the late 90s is an incredible feat, and I cannot express my gratitude enough to the team behind this hack.
