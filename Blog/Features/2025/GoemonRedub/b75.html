Goemon Redub Released

Goe On Then

In what has turned into the fifth piece of new posted in a row on ReCollect64 beginning with the letter "G", Goemon International shared news today that researcher, reverse engineer, and retro games randomiser developer, Abyssonym, had created a Mystical Ninja Starring Goemon Japanese "Redub" patch for the charismatic N64 platformer.

The project aims to restore the original Japanese audio back into the American version of the game to reinstate more authenticity when playing the game. Many have asked for it, and today Abyssonym has answered those pleas with a standalone solution.

A "Redub" Patch has been released for "Mystical Ninja Starring Goemon" by @abyssonym that unlocks the Japanese cutscene voices in the English version of the game.

Mystical Mending

When the game was originally localised for the American market, the internal files were simply renamed or relocated to link to the English version of the movies and audio, but they were not removed from the ROM data at all.

Abyssonym stated:

 

Because there are so many scripts that have to be expanded or relocated, this patch affects a lot of data and is unlikely to be compatible with other patches that alter events. A more flexible method for redubbing MNSG may be available in the Ancient Cave Starring Goemon randomizer in the future."

This patch re-enables all the original audio within the ROM, allowing fans of the series to experience a far more authentic version of the game but retaining the English menus, subtitles, and text.

In order to patch the game, you need to obtain the Mystical Ninja Starring Goemon U ROM with an MD5 of 643CCE1AB06F97E9590241D27E5C2363, apply the mn64redub.bps patch on the patcher site, and the resulting output file will have an MD5 of F9528422BE9E46A4A3F90B884E25E186.

Feel free to grab the BPS patch from the Kofi link, and please consider donating to Abyssonym as a small thank you for their efforts.
