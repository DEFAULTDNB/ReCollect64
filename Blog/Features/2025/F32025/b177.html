F3 2025 Showcase

E3 for Mods & Fangames! ~ Jul 26, 2025

Mod Mayhem

Since 2020 Kaze Emanuar, aka the "Mario Movement Magician" has been hosting a yearly fangame festival, featuring a wide variety of custom creations by fans, ROM hackers, and modders alike.

Spanning Game Boy, NES, Nintendo 64, GameCube, and more, this presentation has quickly become an exciting way to find out about fangames released in the last 12 months, or upcoming releases scheduled to release within the following 12 months.

This article will primarily focus on all the coverage, including the N64-related hacks and mods, and I will endeavour to update links and videos as and when I can:
