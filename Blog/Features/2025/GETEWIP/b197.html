GE Tournament Edition WIP

Never Enough

Expert GoldenEye modder Wreck, aka GoldenEyeMods, has been busily beavering away on something quite special for the GoldenEye 007 and 007 The World is Not Enough fans. GoldenEye 007 Tournament Edition is a massively overhauled ROM hack for GE007 that swaps out all the multiplayer maps in favour of multiplayer arenas from 007 TWINE!

Currently, the mod, which has not been made public and is undergoing a beta testing phase, contains Merchant, Istanbul, Field of Fire, Volcano, Silo Surprise, Submarine, Flashpoint, MI-6 HQ, Castle Thane, and Labyrinth levels. Each of these maps has been masterfully exported from TWINE and imported into the GoldenEye engine, then given collision, weapon placement, and spawn locations, as well as other far more technical things.

I sent out a beta patch of the upcoming #GoldenEye Tournament Edition mod to @THOneTwoSix for testing, and he was able to stream with some of his friends earlier today. You can see a preview of all the current TWINE64 maps being played in his following post. Stay tuned for more. https://t.co/PiGjSiwW8F

Tournament Treats

When the release of this ROM hack comes to pass, I have no doubts that competitive players will gather together from around the world and rinse this for all it's worth on netplay. I noticed in the above video that the character list has the four legendary bonds added back in and alternate costumes for several characters, and there was also a "Pistols Plus" setting in the weapons options, so there will be a variety of fun loadouts to configure to keep the game fresh and inviting for a long while once it's made public.

Wreck's other projects include the ongoing modification GoldenEye X, a port of the GoldenEye levels, weapons and characters into Perfect Dark's updated engine. It's interesting that the levels for TWINE haven't made it into a Perfect Dark ROM hack yet; perhaps once GE:X is more complete, that might happen as a separate project after? Who knows!

As always, I will be keeping my ear to the ground for updates on this one, because as with everything Wreck turns his hand to, you know this is going to be amazing to play.
