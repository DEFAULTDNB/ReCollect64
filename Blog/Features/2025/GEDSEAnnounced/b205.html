GE007: DSE Announced

Explorative Endevour

CptLima has recently announced that he is making a brand new GoldenEye 007 ROM hack entitled Disrupt Surveillance Equipment, and according to his recent posts, it should be nearing completion very soon!

The aim of this hack is to recapture the joy of exploring levels and finding new things. Using around 20 security cameras per level to accomplish this, CptLima's latest project is actually his first fully fledged ROM hack, and he's pouring in the hours to ensure that it's going to be unique and interesting.

Golden Opportunity

With an added emphasis on stealth and detection, this ROM hack could be something very special indeed. Bringing 20 taxing covert missons to players who are up for the ultimate challenge of avoiding and destroying all of the cunningly placed and well-hidden/disguised monitoring systems around the stages, it sounds unambiguously fun!

The addition of extra objectives will also bring another layer of freshness to the 28-year-old title. Judging by the screenshots of the levels, they look to be remixes of existing ones, offering fresh difficulties to overcome for the ultimate spy espionage action.
