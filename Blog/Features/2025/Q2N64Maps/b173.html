Eight More N64 Maps to Play! ~ Jul 19, 2025
Quake II: N64 Jam 2

Map-tastic

Quake II Remastered fans rejoice, for the long awaited, second map pack, containing eight more N64 maps, is now available to download for PC and supported devices!

The pack, a result of the recent Quake 2 N64 Jam 2 event, transforms your Quake II Remaster game with new textures, HUD, and sounds to recreate an authentically N64-themed experience.

 

It’s time to release our very first sequel!

Almost 2 years ago we unleashed our N64 Jam unto the community and now we’re back with 8 brand new maps, including a couple of sequels to maps featured in the first N64 Jam.

That’s not all of course, because we’ve also included an intro, a hub map, animated cutscenes and a boss fight.

These glorious additions come almost two years after the initial N64 SP Map Jam that ran from October 20th to November 24th, 2023 and which yielded nine new N64-themed single-player maps to play from the 1st of December that year.
