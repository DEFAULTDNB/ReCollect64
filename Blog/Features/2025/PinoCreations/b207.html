Ever dreamed of having a Lego effigy of Banjo - I mean Fiddle & Wahooie - to adorn your shelves? Well, thanks to the creative talent of Pino Creations, aka Matteson Pino, you can make this dream a reality with your own 12.3" tall, 925 g statue for a smidge under £235.

 

Bring a dynamic duo to life—brick by brick!

Designed by Matt Pino, the Fiddle & Wahooie Statue stands 12.3” tall and captures all the charm, color, and energy of a certain bear-and-bird pair from a fan-favorite adventure platformer. Built entirely from LEGO bricks, this display model highlights their playful personalities, from Fiddle’s bold stance to Wahooie’s wings mid-flap. Packed with character and nostalgia, this build is perfect for fans of classic adventures and brick-built artistry alike.

I'm a big fan of this build because it perfectly captures the finer details and colouration of the in-game N64 model. The vibrant style and whimsical poses are expertly brought to life and would make a fantastic piece for anyone who adores the bear and bird duo.

With a cost of £232 including tax (and free delivery!), you can preorder this beauty ready for shipping in October, but you'll need to be an intermediately skilled builder to put this 1295-piece build together, because it can get quite fiddly (punn intended) building out the details, such as the tiny golden puzzle piece and the swathes of feathers.

Working out at nearly 18p per piece, this isn't the cheapest build, though this version gets you real, official Lego pieces to build with, so you know you're getting a high-quality build and parts that will not fade or look discoloured or cheap and wonky from day one.
