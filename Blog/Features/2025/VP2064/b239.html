Viewpoint 2064: Final Version Found!

Dumped & Preserved by Hard4Games ~ Nov 25, 2025

Canned Coin-Ops

Viewpoint 2064 was a shoot 'em up game developed by Sammy Corporation, ready for release in late 1999 or possibly early 2000. The game was cancelled, and up until yesterday, only a pre-release prototype that was found in 2020 was available to play.

Friend of the site Hard4Games has put together a comparison video that details the main differences within the two known versions, and I have to say that the final version looks stunning in comparison. Check it out for yourself here:

Perfect Preservation

This is just the latest in a string of quality releases by Hard4Games, who has a track record for finding Nintendo 64 prototypes, dumping them, and preserving them for the world to enjoy.

Previous releases include Mario Party 3, 007: The World is Not Enough, WinBack: Covert Operations, Rugrats, Deadly Arts/G.A.S.P!! Fighters' NEXTream, RakugaKids, Carnivale: Cenzo's Adventure, X'treme Roller, Tonic Trouble, Cubivore, and even some 64DD games, which included the extremely rare Super Mario 64 Disk Version!

Who knows what other long lost treasures might come to the surface one day? I personally hope for Rev Limit and Twelve Tales: Conker 64. I won't hold my breath, but you never know, Dinosaur Planet got preserved in 2021, anything is possible!
