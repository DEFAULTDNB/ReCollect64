OverKart64 Spring Extravaganza 2025

Creative Contest in Progress ~ Jun 11, 2025

Custom Courses

On April the 1st, 2025, OverKart64 announced their second annual spring extravaganza custom map contest for Mario Kart 64. The contest, which runs from April to June, allows modders to pick up the community tools and begin forging new levels for some great prizes!

According to the moderators on Discord, there are two categories for entry: the first is "Architects", which means people looking to create courses, and "Racers", for people who want to obliterate the competition and set the quickest times on these brand new tracks.

Architects stand to win up to $125 USD for first, $75 for second, $50 for third, and $25 for fourth place, and racers can win up to $100 USD for coming first, $50 each for second and third place, and $25 for finishing fourth.

Playable Places

 

The Spring Extravaganza 2025 Map Making contest has completed the first half! 6 brand new custom levels are available to race on.

Great news: with the first 6 courses now available to play, you can check out the locales for yourself! All you need to do is grab the BPS file, the Mario Kart 64 USA ROM (MD5: 3a67d9986f54eb282924fca4cd5f6dff) and head over to Marc Robledo's JS patcher site to produce the patched ROM with an MD5 of 3B1ABA230BC776BBCB7C189858114FDF.

The current selection of courses includes Pokemon Unite Shivre City by Diego Vapy, Lava Fields by Ox, DS Peach Gardens by JCR, Lakitu Lifts by DeadHamster, Rosalina Resort by Kenobi and MK8 Super Bell Subway by Vena-Monterrey. This bunch represents a nice variety with ice, heat, gardens and urban environments to belt around.

Having given them a quick spin on real hardware, I have to say that the quality of these tracks is already top-notch. I will be reserving judgement for now, and reviewing the final ROM, including the winning tracks, as soon as the competition closes at some point in June.

For now, dig out your flashcarts (or use the Ares emulator) and get playing these wonderful tracks; you never know, your best times could be submitable over on the Discord server linked below! The best of luck to everyone!
