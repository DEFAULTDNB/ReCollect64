Smash Remix 2.0.0 Released!

A Smashing Update!

The world's premier modification for Super Smash Bros. is back and bigger than ever. With the addition of five new characters, a multitude of new levels, and a variety of modes, this modification continues to thrive.

With Princess Peach fluttering in with her umbrella, Crash Bandicoot making his debut, Dr Luigi, Roy (Fire Emblem) and last but not least, Lanky Kong round off the fresh faces for this release.

New levels that were also announced include Time Twister, Discovery Falls, LMAO Castle, Pigmask Fortress, Raid Blue, and N64 Remix (a console to fight on) as well as various updates for existing stages.

The previous patch, version 1.5.2, was released in April 2024, so this marks the first update in over 13 months. and it hasn't disappointed.

Fighting Features

Along with the new brawlers, we have new Vs modes such as King of the Hill, Smashketball (score goals or shoot hoops) and a brand new tag team mode for even more multiplayer mayhem.

Finally, a couple of new features were shoehorned into this mammoth trailer, with new items, special zoom for dramatic effect on final smashes and game speed options. Parrying has also been added as well as a new rage mechanic which overpowers your character and causes more damage to your foes!
