To play this game, all you need to do is patch a legitimately obtained 32 MB USA WWF Wrestlemania 2000 *.Z64 ROM with the MD5:D9030CA30E4D1AF805ACCE1BFED988CC, which will produce a 31.7 MB ROM with the MD5: 65AA42A72B273E43C93A6E87694BB398.

What's fantastic about this mod is that you can head on over to AKI Guides Patcher, linked below, and upload the correct ROM to patch. It could not be simpler, and yes, it works perfectly on flashcarts and emulators!

You can also obtain several other ROM hacks at AKI Guide too, including All Japan Pro Wrestling 64 (beta 2), WWF WrestleMania X (1.1 S.K. Edition), and ECW Hardcore House Party Mixtape (1.1 S.K. Edition), so it's a smashing place to fill your boots if you're a wrestling fan!.
