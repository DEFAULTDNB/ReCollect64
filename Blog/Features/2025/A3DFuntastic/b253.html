A3D Funtastics Announced

Timeless Translucents

The Analogue 3D has been out for all of a fortnight, but today Analogue has dropped a rather awesome announcement that they are bringing out a range of eight additional coloured consoles based on the Nintendo 64 Funtastic series colours, ready to ship before Christmas! The colours echo the Y2K era pops of colour that are associated with the iconic translucent coloured devices of a bygone era.

The announcement comes just weeks after ModRetro's M64 was revealed to have around a dozen colours in the works, though those ones will be purchasable separately and will be less focussed on matching the classic funtastic swathe of colours.

Bold & Beautiful

The $299.99 coloured consoles include Jungle, Ice, Fire, Watermelon, Grape, Clear, Smoke and even one to match the limited edition Toys R Us Gold variant N64. To my eyes, these look pretty much spot on in terms of matching the original coloured consoles and will look brilliant when paired with matching controllers. It is however, a little bit of a kick in the teeth for us early adopters though, as we are now required to purchase another entire new console to bathe in the glory of these gorgeous shells. Hopefully Analogue will see fit to release kits so that people who bagged the white or black consoles day one will have options to customise their console.

As predicted in my previous post, 8BitDo are also bringing out controllers to match. These will look superb paired together, and I might just have to snap up a couple more ready for more multiplayer mayhem, in February 2026, when they begin shipping.
