Graslu00 & AdzyIn3D Beat GE007

WITH FISHING RODS! ~ Jul 16, 2025
Hook, Line & Sinker!

Baiting Buddies

Graslu00, speedrunner and Perfect Dark & GoldenEye 007 wunderkind has once again battered the spy shooter with another outlandish controller; this time it's the ASCII Tsurikon fishing rod!

Not only this, but he's now brought along AdzyIn3D to play in parity! Check out the footage below for 180 minutes of speedrunning and cheat unlocking, a spot of GoldenEye 007 and Bass Hunter 2000 multiplayer, and then finally a preview of Brandon's Mod, an upcoming ROM hack for 007: The World Is Not Enough.

Reely Radical

With the rod attached and 1964 GEPD edition set up to play the game, the pair were using mods to allow the reeling action to fire, which, as you would expect, is ridiculously brilliant.

Having bested Bond's most nostalgic game with a wheel, a guitar, and now a fishing rod, I fully expect Perfect Dark to be the next game on the list that will be trounced by the deadly anglers.

For those wondering, Brandon's mod is a work-in-progress, tweaked version of TWINE that aims to make the game far more challenging. By increasing enemy density and altering their timings, the game becomes far more difficult to negotiate through the unchanged missions. A similar mod exists for GoldenEye 007 too.
