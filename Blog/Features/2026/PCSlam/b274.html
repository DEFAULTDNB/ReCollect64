The "Subjected to A Partying" EP will be limited! ~ Jan 23, 2026
Party Cannon Coming to N64

Battle of the Bands

Following in the footsteps of Remute and Belushi Speedball, Party Cannon have announced that they too are releasing an album on a Nintendo 64 cartridge. The Scottish brutal death metal band will be releasing their niche musical stylings as an extremely limited edition Nintendo 64 PAL region cartridge that will feature 4 singles and a plethora of extras:

 

On March 27th, Scotland's favorite brutal party slam band Party Cannon will release their new EP, Subjected To A Partying, via Unique Leader Records. For a preview of Subjected To A Partying, the video for new single "Human Slime (Live at Obscene Extreme 2025)" – taken from the "N64" game cartridge edition (see below for details):

Party Cannon bassist Clankenstein states: "We are going where no slamming death metal band has gone before – Nintendo 64.

That's right, it's time to get N or get out. To celebrate Daryl 'The Frogman' Boyce's first release as our new vocalist, we decided to make it something truly special, and there's nothing more special than a console that’s lifespan ended in 2001. Vominic Stonebones painstakingly designed and ported the cartridge himself. The Frogman sounds like unfettered pressurized sewerage pouring all over these tracks, and we couldn’t be happier with how disgusting these vocals are, it's a new era of Party Slam".

Subjected To A Partying "N64" game cartridge – limited to 100 copies!

Includes:

- All four singles from the EP

- Exclusive band bios and photos

- Hidden pro-shot/mix live video of "Human Slime" live at Obscene Extreme 2025

- 320 x 240 resolution

- 44100 Hz stereo audio

- Exclusive Vominc Stonebones resin mini-figure (unpainted)

- PAL region only

The decision to release on Nintendo 64 is an interesting one because, firstly it's extremely niche, and secondly, a strictly limited 100-piece drop will be incredibly difficult to get hold of and only probably end up in the hands of either avid fans of the band, or scalpers who wish to extort people who love the Nintendo 64.

The Subjected to a Partying EP releases on March 27th, but will you be picking it up? I'm definitely interested in seeing the cart in action, but am I willing to splurge and buy it myself? Time will tell.
