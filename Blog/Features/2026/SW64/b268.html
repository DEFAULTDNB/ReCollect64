Substance Wars 64 Released!

Grand Theft Mushroom

Kaze Emanuar has released his latest transformational Super Mario 64 ROM hack which, almost unbelievably, is a first-person shooting, stock-trading, roguelike which harnesses the almost 30 year old platforming title's engine and transforms it into what can only be described as a full-on gang war-fuelled Grand Theft Auto-like experience!

Like a fever dream, Kaze has turned the edgy 2009 parody YouTube videos where Mario and Luigi take a wrong turn and end up in Vice City into a fully loaded hack that was inspired by the text-based 1984 IBM PC game Drug Wars. Check out the details below!

 

Become a millionaire in this first person, stock trading roguelike.

Please note that the control scheme is made for an original N64 controller - the stick is used for aiming here.

Ride or Die

Fighting gangs, fending off the police, and managing resources and manipulating stock prices through insider trading aside, there is already a competition being held on Kaze's Discord server:
