Light House Coming July

Harbour Masters BK PC Port ~ Jun 14, 2026

Light House

Not to be confused with the Banjo-Kazooie recompilation Banjo: Recompiled by Mr Wiseguy, Light House is the official PC port of Banjo-Kazooie from the Harbour Masters, a team of exquisite developers who use Libultraship to bring us top-quality PC ports with zero AI interference.

The 2-minute teaser-trailer, which was released in the early hours of this morning, indicates a few things. Firstly, a new in-game UI which appears to indicate and possibly track in-game collectables. Secondly, mod support day-one, with Markus Kurko's Nostalgia 64 shown to wonderful effect. And last but not least, and possibly the most potentially exciting feature of all, Archipelago support, which should bring randomisers and could allow for online multiplayer support!

Light House is set to release in July, and the cheekily teased Archipelago support should come "sometime after release." The port will feature all the mod cons of the usual N64-to-PC source ports, including ultra widescreen, modern controls, high FPS and 4K resolutions for PC, Linux and macOS. We could also see ports for the Nintendo Switch and possibly other platforms too.
