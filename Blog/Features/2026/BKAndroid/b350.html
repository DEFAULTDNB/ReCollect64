Dual Screen Duo

No, your eyes do not deceive you; as of this week, Banjo-Kazooie has been ported to Android devices, and this port brings some interesting features, not least of which is second screen support, allowing for the tracking of collectibles in fresh and exciting ways.

The ability to load mods allows for things like BK Reloaded texture packs to work natively, and second screen support is particularly ideal for people with foldable phones like the Galaxy Z Fold series or the Google Pixel Pro Fold, or anyone lucky enough to own a dual-screen gaming devices like the Anbernic RG DS or the Ayaneo Pocket DS, for example.

To enjoy this port, you must supply your own Banjo-Kazooie (U) V1.0 ROM with the MD5 B29599651A13F681C9923D69354BF4A3. Simply drop this ROM onto your Android device and point the APK at it. Please remember that the European and Japanese ROMs are not supported, nor is the 1.1 revision USA ROM.

The More the Merrier

AurelioB appears to have a vested interest in N64 decompilations, with a selection of other titles forked in their repositories. These titles include the kooky tongue-lashing platformers Chameleon Twist and Chameleon Twist 2 and the explosive action-platformer Bomberman Hero.

It would be fantastic to see more N64 games natively ported to the Android ecosystem, because this would make replaying these titles accessible to the vast many people who own Android devices, such as TV sticks, smartphones and tablets.

As usual, I have linked the repository and the the latest release download to the front page of this site for easy tracking. Enjoy!
