GE007:TE New Mode WIP

King of the Hill Incoming! ~ Jun 11, 2026

The Spy Who Loved Hills

The ever-talented veteran GoldenEye 007 modder Wreck, aka GoldenEye Mods, has been hammering away at his Tournament Edition ROM hack to add a fresh mode to multiplayer skirmishes. King of the Hill, or KOTH, is currently being playtested online by Wreck, TH126, and GE6490.

The new mode is still heavily work-in-progress, but Wreck also stated that the mode is being polished up and will have an on-screen timer that indicates the current capture status of the hill: "Next beta patch will feature an on-screen countdown clock (starts at twenty whenever a hill is first entered by a player). There will also be a text pop up when you've successfully defended the Hill, which displays how many you've won."

The Hill is Not Enough

Last time I caught a glimpse of GE007 TE was back in August 2025, when TWINE levels were being imported and tested. The Tournament Edition ROM is specifically crafted to incorporate performance optimisation buffs and graphical corrections that make the multiplayer portion of the original game run better than ever before, which is ideal for competitive multiplayer contests.

The last public release I could locate for GoldenEye 007 Tournament Edition was version 1.04 from 2020, which is hosted over on N64Vault. The latest updates found on Wreck's social channels have suggested that TWINE levels and more modes are heading into the ROM hack, but up-to-date builds and a release date for these latest features to be publicly available are still notably absent.
