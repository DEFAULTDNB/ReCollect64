BK4: Quest for Kurkoins Teased

Five Fresh Screenshots! ~ Feb 10, 2026

Klassic Kurko

Banjo Kazooie ROM hacker/modder extraordinaire and friend of the site Markus Kurko has today unveiled a batch of five brand new screenshots for his upcoming and highly anticipated sequel-of-sorts BK4: Quest for Kurkoins, over on his Patreon page.

Prior to these screenshots there have been several other teaser posts, with the last one being made in August 2025, where a video was shown of Spiral Village. The first-ever mention of BK4: Quest for Kurkoins was made in 2021, where the title was tentatively named BK: Kurkoin Quest, and people were asked to submit their ideas for worlds through a series of contests. Since then, the six main worlds and the hub world have been stitched together into one cohesive package, and BK4: Quest for Kurkoins might at last see the light of day!
