Wave Race 64 DC Port WIP

Water Sports

Stop your grinnin' and drop your linnen, because JNMartin is currently in the throws of porting the aquatic classic Jet Ski racing game to none other than the Sega Dramcast!

With almost perfect looking graphics and sound, that game only suffers slightly in the frame rate department. I'm sure that JNMartin and Falco Girgis plan to iron out all the issues before launch and offer us one of the most eagerly awaited ports before anyone else.

The news was shared by TheSegaGuru on his YouTube channel barely 40 minutes ago, so please take a look at the progress via this gorgeous footage right here:

Maximum Power!

Wave Race 64 has always been a firm Nintendo 64 favourite, and even has it's own informational, amusing and particularly unhinged social media account that is absolutely fanatical about the 1996 game.

Again, the ongoing decompilation effort shows just 65% progress towards the game, and no PC ports or recompilations exist for this title yet. The Dreamcast is yet again getting games ahead of the curve, with a Diddy Kong Racing source port also touted for the system barely a week ago!

Expect me to keep a beady eye on this one, and I will post further news and updates as and when they happen!
