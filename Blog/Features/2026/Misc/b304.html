UPDATED MM PS Vita Port

Released by Rinnegatamante ~ Apr 12, 2026
MM PS Vita Port

Unmasking a Classic

Following on from his previous ports of The Legend of Zelda Ocarina of Time and Perfect Dark, Rinnegatamante has now ported The Legend of Zelda Majoras Mask to the PlayStation Vita consoles.

Using the Harbour Masters 2Ship2Harkinian 1.1.2 (Satoko Charlie) build as a base, which in inself utilised the ZeldaRET's (Zelda Reverse Engineering Team's) decompilation efforts, the game is now running beautifully, and most importatly natively, on Sony's 2011 handheld console as well as the 2013 "consolised" PSTV hardware.

Setup Instructions

Download 2ship2harkinian v.1.1.2 for PC.

Follow its instructions to generate mm.o2r.

Extract 2s2h.zip from the Release tab in ux0:data.

Place mm.o2r and 2ship.o2r in ux0:data/2s2h.

Install the vpk from the Release tab.

Porting Possibilities

Going forward, Rinnegatamante has stated that this port aims to have some parity with the latest releases of 2Ship2Harkinian for PC, which currently sits some eight revisions higher at 4.0.2, aka Keiichi Charlie, at the time of writing, so there are plenty of improvements and fixes to come to the PS Vita version yet.

He also stated that Starship (Star Fox 64) will be ported at somepoint, however there are problems regarding the "display swapchain" and therefore Ghostship (Super Mario 64), and possibly Spaghetti Kart (Mario Kart 64) will see ports to PS Vita sooner than Star Fox 64, as they are now the focus.

UPDATE: 16/04/26 - 2ship2harkinian for Vita v.1.1 is out. Control issues fixed as well as several other bugs and glitches.
