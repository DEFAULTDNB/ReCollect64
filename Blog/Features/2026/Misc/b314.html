Buck Bumble's Return?

Black & Yellow

For the past few days, ArgonautGames, the publisher's with previous creations that include Croc and Starfox, have been teasing us all across their social channels. Using Wiz Khalifa's "Black and Yellow" and a particular striped concentric pattern of rings as a clue. The last tease uses the infamously banging Buck Bumble theme song, and features an HD logo and image of the titular Bombus.

After the third tease they announced that none other than Buck Bumble would be the new wholesome mascot for 4/20 (if you get what that means), and that there would be more announcements coming up soon. With their recent Croc: Legend of the Gobbos HD Remaster release, perhaps Buck Bumble will soon follow suit and get a fresh outing on modern platforms, though nothing is set in stone yet.

Better BEElive

I for one want to believe that a HD remaster, or remake, of of the cyborg bumblebee is in the pipeline because the game was cult classic and a fun game that I can definitely recommend people play, even if it's lacking some much needed features that could (in my opinion) elevate its stature even more.

I look forward to finding out more, and I'm hoping that if a Buck Bumble remaster does get announced, it will have some serious tweaks and quality of life updates to put it on par with the quality of the Nightdive Studio remasters we have been lucky enough to get in recent years. I really hope that it's not just a slap dash upscale with no attention taken to ensure it plays at least as well as the original game (I'm looking at you GoldenEye 007's 2023 release by Code Mystics).
