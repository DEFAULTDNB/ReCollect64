Crate Expectations

CptLima of GoldenEye: Disrupt Surveillance Equipment fame has recently been plugging his latest ROM hack idea, the relatively off-the-wall "GoldenCrate", a mod that promises more destroyed crates than a UPS depot.

This ROM hack, which is in its very early stages, will offer more challenges across the various existing campaign maps by adding in new set pieces that require you to seek out new approaches to quashing hidden enemy threats and ticking off fresh mission objectives.

One of my goals in #GoldenCrate007 is to rearrange the flow of missions to provide a wholly different experience. Expect a lot of tough guards - and some new objectives mixed in, too.

Boxed Off

CptLima recently asked the public, "If one mission in GoldenEye could be improved by adding more crate guards, which one do you suggest it could be?". I replied, "Train. Make it rammed with crates and guards. I want 64-bit claustrophobia." His response was "Done deal. Even the crates will have crates."

I always look forward to content created by speedrunners and glitch hunters, because they always produce the most entertaining and intricate ROM hacks and mods. This one already looks pretty streamlined yet unorthodox, but you never know; it could turn out to be another absolute classic!

There is no set release date for GoldenCrate 007, but I'm extremely hopeful that it will release on or before GoldenEye 007's 30th anniversary, which will be celebrated on the 23rd of August, 2027.
