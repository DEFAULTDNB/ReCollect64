OverKart64 Winter Extravaganza

Get Revved up!

Dead Hamster and the OverKart 64 Community are hosting their annual custom Mario Kart 64 track building contest, and they are looking for new and exciting entrants to produce some fresh tracks for the now 29-year-old racing classic.

This will be the third annual event, but this time around, to spice things up, there's going to be a theme to the entries. "Wonders of the world" is the design brief, and this opens up a lot of scope for people to capture some of the planet's natural, modern or ancient, iconic landmarks!

Annual Awards

The creative contest is due to end around the beginning of April, so there is plenty of time to take up tools such as Tarmac 64 to get in good stead to compete for those cash prizes. If you think you have what it takes, then please enter; the more the merrier!

Following the creative side of the contest, the competitive side will immediately kick in and racers will be tasked with setting the best times over the newly crafted terrains.
