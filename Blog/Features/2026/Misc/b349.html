The Nintendo 64 has remained a rock-solid mainstay in my life since I first got one in March of 1997. I remember coming out of school and my mum had been to Toys R Us to collect it for me. I opened the boot of the car and laid eyes on it for the first time. The feeling was absolute ecstasy. As you can tell by ReCollect64's ethos and frequent updates, I love the machine and everything about it, and that affection has never wavered or waned in three decades.
