2 WCW nWo Recomps Released!

A Flair for Recompilation

Not even one week since the last recomp landed, not one but two more AKI-wrestling-based recompilations have hit our shores with an almighty thud. This time it's the turn of WCW - nWo Revenge and WCW vs. nWo World Tour, and these wrestling titles have been lovingly recompiled by Jesse Carey.

Both recomps are in deeply beta states, though both show excellent signs of growing into firm fan favourites once all the wrinkles have been ironed out. Each feature RT64 integration for high-resolution graphics and intact effects, emulated SRAM saves, local multiplayer for up to 4 players, widescreen and rumble support!

The Big Show

In order to try out these recomps, you can head to the links below (or the recomp links on the front page of this blog) and download the latest version available under the releases tabs. Using the WCW - nWo Revenge (U) ROM with an MD5 of E1711A2511394B9357B5F1AC8CA5CC17BD674836, and the WCW vs. nWo World Tour (U) ROM with an MD5 of 5AD2D8359058C8BB71F08E3D3433B7A50D3BB645, you can enjoy both releases by simply dragging and dropping the ROMs into their respective folders and running their launchers.

Features such as hack/mods and unlocked/high framerates will be coming to these projects in the near future; however, as betas, they do not have all the functions available just yet. For now, we can all enjoy the summer sun and soak up all of the bountiful wrestling action we have before us.
