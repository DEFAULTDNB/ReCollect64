SM64 PS Vita Port

One N-sixty More

Following on from his most recent port of The Legend of Zelda Majora's Mask, Rinnegatamante has now brought a native Super Mario 64 to the PlayStation Vita consoles, by utilising the Harbour Masters Ghostship 1.0.2 (Dutchman Charlie), which is the latest release.

As well as remappable controls, stable fram rates and enhancements, this port will also be compatible with mods, which means it will be possible to swap characters and employ HD texture packs too. Expect more updates to enhance stability a squash any remaining minor bugs to follow.

Description:

Ghostship is a sourceport of Super Mario 64 based off the decompilation efforts of the game. It features several enhancements and mods support. The Vita port is based on version 1.0.2 (Dutchman Charlie) with bugfixes from the in-development branch.

Requirements:

- libshacccg.suprx

- Game Data Files: N64
