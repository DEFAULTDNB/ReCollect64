BK/BT on Evercade Nexus!

Kazooie & Tooie Double Pack!

Blaze has today announced their latest cartridge-based retro device, the 5.89” IPS-screened Evercade Nexus will land in October with a price of £169.99, and most intriguingly of all for N64 fans; it will come with both Banjo-Kazooie and Banjo-Tooie as a double pack cartrige. The last time Banjo-Tooie was re-released was in October of 2024, for the Nintendo Switch Online Service.

The cartridge, which is numbered "53", has a Banjo's blue backpack coloured complexion and is PEGI rated 7, will be released alongside the console, and in addition a special "64 Edition" of the handheld will also be available. Preorders open tomorrow, so get in quick if you want one!

 

Here's your look at the Evercade Nexus announcement trailer for the upcoming retro gaming handheld console. The Evercade Nexus handheld for Evercade gaming cartridges features an ultra-bright 5.89” IPS screen, dual analogue sticks, wireless headphone support, and the new EverSync local multiplayer function. The Evercade Nexus comes with the new Banjo-Kazooie Double Pack physical cartridge in the box with Rare's Banjo-Kazooie and Banjo-Tooie on one physical cart. The Evercade Nexus will be available in October 2026, and pre-orders will open on April 1, 2026.

Additionally, the Evercade Nexus 64 edition will also be available. This limited edition version of the Evercade Nexus will be available from Funstock, with pre-orders open now.
