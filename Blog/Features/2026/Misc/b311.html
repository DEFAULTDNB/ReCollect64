Mega Man 64 Recomp Released

Mega Machine

Based on RaieFox's decompilation efforts, their first stable release of the Mega Man 64 recompilation has now been made public. Prior to this, the unstable Alpha (V0.1.0) was released at the beginning of May.

With high framerate and widescreen support, as well as modern controller mapping, this recomp is now the 13th publicly released recompilation of a decompiled game that utilises the N64: Recompiled codebase to statically recompile each title.

Mega Marvels

In order to play the game, all you will need is the original, lawfully obtained dump of the Mega Man 64 (U).Z64 ROM with an MD5 of 3620674ACB51E436D5150738AC1C0969. Dragging and dropping the ROM into the extracted release and running the executable will guide you through the setup process, and get you playing.

The game is 100% completable, but there are some known graphical issues such as the geometry's seams, particle effects and cutscene desync, but essentially, it's fully playable and can now be enjoyed by anyone with a Windows, macOS, Linux or even a Steam Deck! No doubt updates will follow to polish this recomp up further.

If you would like to see what the game currently looks like in action, warts and all, then please check out the gameplay video released By Video Game Esoterica, which demonstrates the current state of the port.
