Delightful Donations

This years Game Jam helped raise ober $3300 for charity, which in itself is no mean feat. The winners, Team Ultrarare (lambertjamesd, Pyroxene, SapphireOnze, beta_dynast, terzdesign), will get to chose the charity, or charities, they wish to gift the donation to in the coming days.

A massive thank you to all who entered, and all who pledged money to help a worthy cause. Thanks to you, we have a plethora of fresh games to play and a fantastic scene-funded contribution being made to good causes.
