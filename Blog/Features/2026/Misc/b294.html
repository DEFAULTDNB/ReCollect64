Smash Remix 2.0.1 Released!

Incremental Improvements

Back in June, Smash Remix 2.0.0 was released with a whopping 5 new characters, new modes and a whole lot more. Today, 2.0.1 has been released to rebalance the game and pile on a load more content for the already expansive ROM hack.

2.0.1 includes a massive array of alterations for such a minor update, and the Smash Remix team has once again put together a demonstration of the visual and technical improvements for your viewing pleasure:

Fighting Fit

As well as gorgeously reimported and optimised levels, character skeleton updates, additional costumes, fresh music added and general tweaking, this update notably includes a rebalanced Remix single-player mode, improved CPU players and background music and SFX volume controls.

Tug of war has also been added to the VS Remix modes, making for a new way to play against friends in longer, more tactical battles.
