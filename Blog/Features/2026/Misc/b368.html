Mischief Makers Recomp Released

Making Trouble

Out of nowhere, Thiago Lira has dropped a fresh N64 recomp by way of Touble Makers: Recompiled. Yuke! Yuke! Trouble Makers (or Go-Go Trouble Makers), which is known as Mischief Makers in the USA and Europe, is a 2D side-scrolling adventure by Treasure and Enix that was one of the only true two-dimensional games on the system.

This AI-facilitated recompilation takes the core game we know and love and sprinkles in 60 FPS and 16:9 widescreen options, with correct sound and higher resolution graphics. Crucially, Claude's assistance is noted within the repository, and the core decompilation project by Drahsid is cited the source of the ongoing codebase, which currently displays just 41.06% completion overall.

Just for Yuke

To play this recompilation, you will need the USA version 1.1 z64 ROM of Mischief Makers with an MD5 of 2EE917366F64A06472D7622A2A05990E. The revision 1 version of the ROM contained behind-the-scenes bug fixes and optimisations, more than anything else, so it's great that this version is the one being used here. The project is linked on the front page of this site (prefixed with "AI:") as well as at the bottom of this article.
