Star Fox 64 Remake Incoming

Visually Stunning ~ May 7, 2026

Barrel Rolling Again!

Last night, Nintendo sprung huge news of an impromptu Direct presentation that would feature nothing other than Star Fox. Riding high on his supporting cameo appearence within the Super Mario Galaxy Movie, Nintendo has today announced that the Nintendo 64 version of Star Fox, known as Star Fox 64 in the USA and Japan, or Lylat Wars in PAL regions, has been completely remade for the Nintendo Switch 2!

With jaw-dropping visuals overlaying the familiar classic level designs, enemies and vehicles, this 1:1 remake delivers a multitude of improvements in terms of additional cinematics, altered dialogue, new modes, challenges and more. Check out the full trailer and gameplay from last night's presentation below:

McCloud Rides, Again

The last entry in the Star Fox saga released a decade ago on Nintendo's ill-fated Wii U console. Star Fox Zero was an attempt at a reimagining of Star Fox 64 with branching levels and additional motion controls; however, the game fell short with fans due to the steep learning curve and annoying control systems. This entry looks to be a complete remake of the original game, taking what made it great and simply spiffing it up visually and with some much needed fresh content for current generations to enjoy.

I recently re-re-replayed Lylat Wars/Star Fox 64 for the umpteenth time in order to review it. My only real criticism was that the game felt a little short, even with 15 stages to smash through and medals to gather. This remake looks to bring additional hours of fun to the title, through achievement-style challenges, new modes like king of the hill, 8 player online multiplayer and even more cutscenes that fill in more of the lore leading up to and during the battle against Andross across the Lylat system.

The biggest question remains: will "On-Foot" mode appear in the remake? I genuinely hope so, because it was such a treat to see your furry crew skirmishing around outside of their trusty vehicles. We should find out extremely soon, because the game releases on June 25th, 2026!
