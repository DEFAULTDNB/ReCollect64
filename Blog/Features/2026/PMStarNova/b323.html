Paper Mario Star Nova Released

By NicTube ~ May 5, 2026

News Paper

This morning solo game developer, creator and ROM hacker NicTube released his latest and greatest Paper Mario overhaul modification to date: Paper Mario: Star Nova.

As a stepping stone towards his game development ambitions and serving as a living portfolio of his work, NicTube is single-handedly modding Paper Mario 64 to craft some of the finest 2D RPG-based ROM hacks to date, without the use of AI and without any decompilation sources to hand either.

 

Paper Mario: Star Nova is a full-scale story-based Paper Mario 64 mod made by me (NicTube).

NicTube's previous ROM hacks Paper Mario: Sacred Trio (2023) and Paper Mario: Mystical Mansion (2025) are two slightly smaller mods which have largely flown under the radar of the average Nintendo 64 enjoyer, but Star Nova looks to change that with a massive amount of content and

Sadly, this ROM hack will only work on emulators, and the author has no plans to make the game playable on flashcarts in the forseeable future. The reason for this is that emulators are far more forgiving than real hardware environments, and the mods used would need to be reworked to fit within the confines of a cartridge-based game.

Reguardless, this is another stellar modification of a classic Nintendo 64 game, which you can enjoy on PC, tablets, or almost any emulator-capable devices.
