GE007 VR Port WIP

For Your Eyes Only

Today I spotted something cool, the beginnings of a project by Reddit user Frank__West, aka @McDudeMan, that aims to port GoldenEye 007 directly to VR by using Unity and VRChat as a basis. Taking the extracted ROM data, including level geometry, objects, character models and weapons, the VR-look is certainly a head-turning evolution for the 1998 FPS game.

The project has been in action for just a few weeks, but progress is going slow and steady with multiple levels touted to be in the works, but Dam and Bunker II both seem to be pretty much fully imported and workable, with Surface and Depot in the works next.

 

First Update (2 weeks ago):

I was able to get all of the level data out of the ROM and port it over into a VR environment. It's really cool seeing everything in VR and feels so nostalgic.

One day I want to get it to be more of a functional game but I have to start learning how to program that stuff in.

if there's any interest in this then definitely will like keep working at making it a full game. Otherwise my plans are to port all the other levels over as well.

Second Update (Today):

I got Bunker II ported over last night with a few things still needing to be done but overall it is coming along nicely. I still have a bunch of things I want to add but so far it looks pretty good I think. lighting still needs work.

This footage was taken from a Quest 2.

Can't wait to eventually have a proper trailer for the whole project.

Note: "I'm starting Surface tonight"

Third Update (Today):

Hey guys thought I would share this here with the GE community. I am working on bringing GoldenEye and eventually Perfect Dark into VR as well as mobile/desktop. Everything is is being taken from the original ROM and brought over.

This footage was taken while running on a Quest 2 in hardware.

Would love to hear your thoughts and suggestions! This is just a fun project I am doing.

If anyone is interested in possibly helping or even doing some play testing feel free to reach out via DMs or discord @McDudeMan as I would love for some feedback from people as I work on this.

Note: "Working on Depot tonight."

Virtual Victory

The above footage is an incredible leap from purely emulating the ROM in a VR-capable emulator, such as Dolphin with Wii64, for example, and demonstrates the nostalgia gamers still have for the game after all this time.

If the developer can manage to implement all features, such as doors, breakable objects, true 6DOF and 1:1 controls, while being rebuilt entirely in Unity, this iteration of GoldenEye 007 could be something special, and could you imagine if multiplayer support gets added too?!

At the time of writing the developer has stated that he is indeed using VRChat to "provide a large audience on almost any platform" which is a little concerning because typically VRC mods are never incredible and various GoldenEye 007 maps already exist for the free-to-play social platform. There is no standalone playable demo or APK to download or sideload yet, but McDudeMan has hinted that once GoldenEye 007 is complete, Perfect Dark will follow, so perhaps purely single-player spy experiences in VRC are the focus, for now anyway.
