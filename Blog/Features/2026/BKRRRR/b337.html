BK Riverside Ruins Released

A New Banjo-Kazooie ROM Hack ~ Jun 4, 2026

Collaborative Effort

A fresh one-level ROM hack for Banjo-Kazooie has just hit the ground running this week by way of the hackpack community; an entire posse of over 20 enthusiastic Banjo-Kazooie modders, including The Bonsai Treehouse, Star!, Sorra1, Kurkomark, APT, YoomTah, SuperZambezi, ThatCowGuy, Morrigan Aensland-Hill, Drakel, TheSourOG, BanjoFreak64, RetroNuva10, Riposte, Dean Pat, Syeo, Shuggy, Wedarobi, Protocol, Unalive, and Perez.

Banjo-Kazooie: Riverside Ruins is a roughly half an hour-long jaunt set in the bowels of a lush, tropical jungle, where you get to explore the ruins of an ancient civilisation. It features 11 jiggies to discover, 100 notes to collect, as well as two honeycombs to find!

Patch 'N' Play

If you would like to try out this brand new mini-adventure, all you need to do is patch your legally obtained Banjo-Kazooie (U).z64 ROM (MD5: B29599651A13f681C9923D69354BF4A3) using the xDelta patch provided, over on Marc Robledo's ROM patching site. The resulting ROM will have an MD5 hash of 3FFB2901E2D69F93D35D18465F7072DB.

Though Riverside Ruins is a quick single-level experience, I wonder if the Hackpack community is using the "Rare Relay" moniker as an all-encompassing project that will eventually see more levels created by the 20+ strong team. With so many talented individuals working towards one combined uber ROM-hack, the possibilities are endless!
