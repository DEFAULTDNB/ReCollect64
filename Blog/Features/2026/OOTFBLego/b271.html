The Final Battle ~ Jan 16, 2025
New OOT LEGO Revealed!

Bricking it

Following on from their previous teaser, LEGO's latest creation within the Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time crossover has been leaked. This time it's a rather awesome diorama/playset in the shape of 77093 "The Final Battle", featuring Gannon and Link hashing it out across 1003 pieces!

It should be noted that the information has not been revealed officially, and the official Lego site has not been updated with this information at the time of writing.

Building a Series

The kit is set to retail for $130 or £99.99 and will release on March the 1st, according to Brick Tap, who is widely considered a reliable source for LEGO leaks within the fan community. Containing three mini figures, including Link, Zelda and Ganondorf, as well as a Megaton hammer, recovery hearts, Navi and a whole bunch of action-posable flames and special effects, this set looks fantastic for recreating the iconic end-of-game battle.

Compared to the Great Deku Tree kit that arrived in September 2024 for £259.99, the price is staggeringly enticing, and as the second kit in the series, I'm sure fans of Ocarina of Time will want to preorder this as soon as official details land.

I wonder what other ideas Nintendo and LEGO secretly have up their sleeves? I'm personally hedging my bets for news of something Majoras Mask-related being released at some point in or around 2027. As a massive fan of Skull Kid and the titular mask, I'm on the edge of my seat and looking forward to the day that, hopefully, happens!

What's more likely is some kind of tie-in to the upcoming Legend of Zelda movie (set to release in 2027), which would make more sense but it would break my heart if no Majora's Mask sets ever come to exist.
