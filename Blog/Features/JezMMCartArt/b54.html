Incredible N64 Art Spotted!

N64VEMBER Rocked! ~ Dec 7, 2024
JezMM's Cart Art

Cart & Craft Exhibition

Over the course of November, Jeremy Shaw, a.k.a. JezMM, put together the hashtag, #N64VEMBER, showcasing some incredible daily drawings focused on feminine characters from games on the Nintendo 64 system, so of course I gave them a follow!

The 30-piece project features characters from a whole host of titles, including Super Smash Bros., Jet Force Gemini, Banjo-Kazooie, Rayman 2, Pilotwings 64, Mischief Makers, Mario Tennis, GoldenEye, Paper Mario, Sin & Punishment, and more! Each character is instantly recognisable by their distinct looks and themed attire.

The collection, which is now available to view in its entirety on JezMM's personal site, displays the gorgeous artwork with characters such as Natalya Simonova, Vela, Samus Aran, Peach, Daisy, Berri from Conker, Katt Monroe, Akari Hayami, Ayumi Stewart, Adon from Turok 2, and even a banana fairy from DK64!
