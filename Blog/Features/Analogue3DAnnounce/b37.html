UPDATED Analogue 3D Announced

FPGA N64 ~ Oct 16, 2024
Analogue 3D Announced

Refreshed Hardware

Analogue has finally announced their long awaited reimagining of the N64 by way of the Analogue 3D!

Almost one year ago, to the day, the FPGA system was announced with scant details, today they have finally lifted the shroud and given us an idea of the hardware to come, the release date, and the expected feature set!

 

Introducing Analogue 3D. A reimagining of the N64.

In 4K resolution. Perhaps the greatest multiplayer system of all time. Analogue 3D is the worlds first 100% compatible with every original N64 game ever made. Region Free. Bluetooth LE. Dual-band Wifi. Four original-style controller ports. Entirely new, next generation Analogue hardware featuring 3D OS. Engineered entirely in FPGA. No Emulation.

The games aren’t the past—they’re now. Analogue 3D gives you the N64 you need, brought back pure and ready.

Full announcement here: http://analogue.co/3D

Available for preorder Oct 21st, 8am PDT.

Features:

  • 4K Resolution.
  • Region free.
  • 100% compatibility.
  • Bluetooth LE.
  • Dual Band Wifi.
  • Four controller ports.

The Future of N64

Analogue 3D represents a milestone in video game preservation. It’s not just a reimagining of the N64—it’s the first fully compatible, hardware-accurate recreation, fully compatible with the entire N64 game library. After nearly four years of dedicated FPGA engineering, Analogue 3D overcomes the limitations of software emulation—no input lag, no graphic or audio inaccuracies, no timing or frame rate problems. This is the N64, perfected and true to its core.

Also announced was the $39.99 8BitDo 64 controller, which is the wireless controller built for the system and features the classic buttons in a more modern package. I personally will be hoping to use the NSO N64 controller, along with the Blue Retro adaptor, in order to replay my games!

With preorders starting next week and a price of just $249.99, the system is a lot more affordable than I initially thought it might be!

I will be jumping on this day one if possible. I have been waiting for something like this to come along for a very long time, and with the state of the Polymega's ordering system and pricing, it's simply never been worth the aggravation trying to get one just to play N64 games on.

UPDATE: 18/03/2025 - Analogue have admitted that the release date has slipped back towards Q3 2025 (July), and that you can cancel your preorder at any time.

 

Analogue 3D was originally available for preorder at the end of 2024 scheduled to ship Q1 2025.

Analogue 3D shipping is delayed (as of March 18th, 2025) and is now shipping by July 2025. We are working hard to get your 3D order in hands asap and appreciate your patience. If you have an open preorder no action is needed.

As always, if you’d like to cancel your pre-order at any time, Analogue has always had a no questions asked, 100% refund policy for pre-orders.

Before shipping your order, we will email you one week in advance to ensure we have an up-to-date shipping address. You may cancel your order at any time for a full refund before shipment.

Please feel free to contact Analogue Support at any time.

It's nice to have some meaningful communication from the company, as their last word on the Analogue 3D was five months ago, in October 2024.
