#101 - Nintendo 64 Rulez

On July 1st, SuperPod Saga released their latest entry in their long-running podcast series, titled "Nintendo 64 Rulez".

Kicking off the show, host Aaron Klaassen and co-host Gerry Thomas introduce the guests Jiggylookback and Quest64, aka Mike. Barely 8 minutes into the 2-hour show, and given free reign to plug anything he likes, Mike proceeds to shout out none other than ReCollect64, bigging up this very blog, as well as describing existing facets and upcoming features of the ReCollect64 Collection app site!

The podcast features some hilarious out-of-pocket content and some really interesting anecdotes when it comes to the world of N64. I have to say it was a great listen, and I will definitely be bookmarking the site to check out more of their content!

I would like to personally thank Mike and Superpod Saga for the recognition on air; it's humbling, and thank you to all my readers for your support in getting this site off the ground.
