ShadPS4 Emulator runs Turok!

Time to Hunt! ~ July 11, 2024
Turok on ShadPS4

New Generation of Emulation

With the N64 turning 28 this year, it's only fitting that the games we know and love are still effortlessly turning heads and being played in 2024.

A recent PS4 emulator known as ShadPS4 by George Moralis and his team has come on leaps and bounds since it began life in July 2023, and today it has come to light that N64 classic Turok Dinosaur Hunter is now playable on the fledgling PS4 emulator!

ShadPS4 gives PC owners the ability to emulate PS4 titles on their powerful rigs, which is an incredible feat and is the latest of Sony's home consoles to be emulated since RCPS3 brought PS3 games to emulation enthusiasts beginning in 2011. RCPS3 is now a sensational and well-rounded emulator, giving hope that PS4 emulators like ShadPS4 can follow suit.

Strength to Strength

ShadPS4 V0.1.1 WIP is the latest version of the PlayStation 4 emulator, which now sees users getting into menus and even in-game in several cases. 2D games such as Sonic Mania and Record of Lodoss are virtually fully playable, and Bloodborne is slowly progressing into menus and I'm sure it will eventually get in-game soon, though depending on the hardware you're using to emulate Sony's PS4 console, you may have relatively low frame rates until it matures further.

Given the emulator's relatively young pedigree, it's still amazing to see N64 games hitting the main stage, and it's even more impressive to see that PS4 games are playable on PC!

The next question is: do similarly remastered N64 games such as Shadowman, Doom 64, Turok 2 Seeds of Evil, and Turok 3 Shadow of Oblivion run too?

I will definitely look into this more!
