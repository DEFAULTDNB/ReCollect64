ReCollect64 Chats No. 36

ChameleonTwist1 ~ Jul 30, 2025
@ChameleonTwist1

Welcome to ReCollect64 chat! Let's kick-off this Q&A by describing your presence on social media for people:

Hey I’m Chadwick, owner of the Chameleon Twist X account, a long time gamer, hobbyist, and also a small business owner Conflux Gaming. On social media I try to advertise my company, promote the community, and share what I love about gaming to others so they can enjoy it too. The Chameleon Twist page specifically is for sharing that game. It’s my favorite game, it’s extremely short but left an impact on growing up. N64 happened to also be my favorite console.

When did you get into gaming, any formative recollections?

As a kid, my mom was a gamer back then and would regularly kick our butts in Mortal Kombat Trilogy. She got my my first consoles and games, the Gameboy Color, Pokemon Blue, N64 and Mario Kart 64.

What does your retro gaming collection look like?

Mostly N64, and a slowly growing collection of other games / consoles. I have many of the Classic systems that BBC are out a few years ago, as well as my GameBoy games.

Do you have a favourite console?

By far the N64.

What games do you play regularly?

I play all kinds of games, but I’ve spent the most time with my retro games like Diddy Kong Racing & Zelda, then modern games like Destiny 2 & Street Fighter 6.

What snacks/drinks keep you fuelled up for gaming?

I’m pretty healthy I actually have been on a carnivore diet for a few years. In the first 9 months I lost 115 lbs, and I’ve maintained that lost weight, so basicly coffee with half/half, GFuel, beef, and eggs.

Do you have a prized gaming possession?

I have a Japanese CIB Chameleon Twist, and a couple CIB N64 consoles.

Do you have any gaming unicorns left to obtain?

I would love to complete the N64 collection, but I also want a CIB Chameleon Twist 2 game.

Are you looking forward to any games in particular, regarding the recent N64 Recomp projects?

Diddy Kong Racing being Recomped or decomped I think would be a huge thing. It’s the perfect base for a new Kart Racer and I alwayed wished there were more games like it. Mario Kart just isn’t quite the same.

You’re ordering pizza, you get three toppings, what’s your combo?

Hawaiian with beef.

What’s your most expensive/thrifty retro purchase to date?

Probably my original Pokemon card collection. I completed a set of 151 Pokemon. For games, just my N64 Consoles. I do also own Worms Armageddon and Conker’s.

Lastly: How many consoles do you own?

I own 15, everything from N64, GameBoy, to PS1-PS5, and some classic consoles.

Thank you very much for taking part in the ReCollect64 Q&A chats!
