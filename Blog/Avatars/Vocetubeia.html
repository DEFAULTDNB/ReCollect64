Welcome to ReCollect64 chat Artur! Many may already know you online as @Vocetubeia but lets begin with a little about yourself:

I like to spread the word about the N64, its games, low-poly graphics and everything else about it.

When did you get into gaming, any formative recollections?

Got my first console at the age of 10. It was an NES clone so I played a lot of Mario, Mega Man and Contra. I also played Fantastic Adventures of Dizzy a lot. It was a fascinating game, probably the one I rented the most in my childhood.

What does your retro gaming collection look like?

It's like a very organized wall of N64, Super Famicom and other beautiful game boxes but also lots of other stuff in random places around the house (I'm not happy about this part).

Do you have a favourite console?

Yes! It's none other than the N64.

What games do you play regularly?

Star Fox 64, Banjo-Kazooie, Super Mario 64 and Mystical Ninja Starring Goemon. I play new games too. But these are the ones I keep coming back to.

Do you have a prized gaming possession?

A set of 10 japanese Club Nintendo soundtrack CDs. Also, a fullset of Goemon games on PS1. Every single release of every title. All of them complete with an obi, some still sealed.

Do you have any gaming unicorns left to obtain?

Nothing right now. I haven't bought anything recently and there's no space for my collection to grow.
