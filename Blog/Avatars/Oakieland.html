ReCollect64 Chats No. 32

Oakieland AKA Facts_About_BK! ~ May 29, 2025

Welcome to ReCollect64 chat! Let's kick-off this Q&A by describing your presence on social media for people:

I go by "Facts About BK" or "Oakieland" on most social medias. On Facts About BK I usually distribute niche facts about the Banjo-Kazooie series and include discoveries and findings. Our recent biggest discovery was finding the Banjo-X model of Banjo in a prototype of Viva Pinata. OlieGamerTV had found it after fixing a developer tool and I was able to rip it then distribute on SketchFab.

When did you get into gaming, any formative recollections?

I always played games. The first video games I owned was Yoshi's Island: Super Mario Advance 3 and Godzilla: Domination! for the GameBoy Advance. I starting playing on my dad's Nintendo 64 when I was a toddler. I almost exclusively played Banjo-Tooie and Turok 2: Seeds of Evil because of the dinosaurs.

What does your retro gaming collection look like?

My retro game collection is mostly just Banjo-Kazooie merchandise and the series in a few languages.

Do you have a favourite console?

Nintendo Switch. It has all the games I loved on GameCube minus one or two. It has Spyro, Mario + Rabbids, Bugsnax, Bug Fables, and Super Mario Odyssey is still one of the best 3D platformers I've ever played.

What games do you play regularly?

Recently? R.E.P.O.

Overtime? Dungeons & Dragons. I've been a forever Dungeon Master since 2021. I have a game every week. At most I've hosted 3 games at a time with separate groups (on/off Fridays and Saturdays). My longest session to date was 14 hours long. Though I've shrunk it down to 2 games, one group every Friday and one group every other Saturday, each only 4-5 hours. Current campaign for both is Rime of the Frostmaiden.

What snacks/drinks keep you fuelled up for gaming?

Crunchy Flamin' Hot Limón Cheetos and a Sugar-Free Monster.

Do you have a prized gaming possession?

A metal pre-order keychain of Banjo that was exclusive to the UK and Canadian markets when Banjo-Kazooie 1st released on the Nintendo 64.

Do you have any gaming unicorns left to obtain?

PTOPOnline's Banjo-X prototype that they reported on. The data and information even if its an unbootable prototype is enticing to me.

Are you looking forward to any games in particular, regarding the recent N64 Recomp projects?

Banjo-Tooie. I wish to see modding efforts go towards that.

You’re ordering pizza, you get three toppings, what’s your combo?

A local pizza place closed down but they had a really good pizza. It was Bacon, Parmesan Garlic Fries topped with a in-house fry sauce. It has been four years and I still think about it every once in a while.

What’s your most expensive/thrifty retro purchase to date?

The Japanese-exclusive Tooty plush made by Takara. It was $400 USD on eBay and now sits in a Banjo-Tooie coffee cup on a shelf.

Lastly: How many consoles do you own?

1.

Some fantastic answers! Thank you so much for your time and for being part of ReCollect64's Q&A chats!

As well as currently working on BKGR: Reimagined, a wonderful project that aims to bring the 2D GBA title into 3D for PC, Oakieland also runs Facts_About_BK, which posts interesting and unique Banjo-Kazooie-related facts and trivia, along with the latest updates from the BKGR: Reimagined project.

If you're into Banjo-Kazooie in any way, shape or form, then I highly recommend checking out Oakieland's socials and project pages; there is some cracking content to be found!
