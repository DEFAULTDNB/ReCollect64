ReCollect64 Chats No. 44

Nintendo Brasil Wiki ~ Nov 27, 2025

Welcome to ReCollect64 chat NBW! Let's kick-off this Q&A by describing a little about yourself.

Alexsandro Magalhães: Ator, dublador, comunicador, criador de conteúdo e streamer. apaixonado pelos jogos antigos que marcaram minha infância e minha vida.

Alexsandro Magalhães: Actor, voice actor, communicator, content creator, and streamer. Passionate about the old games that marked my childhood and my life.

When did you get into gaming, any formative recollections?

Comecei a jogar video games aos 5 anos de idade. Meu primeiro jogo foi Donkey Kong Country, em 1998. E o primeiro jogo que terminei foi o bootleg Sonic 4 para SNES, por volta de 1999/2000

I started playing video games when I was 5 years old. My first game was Donkey Kong Country, in 1998. And the first game I finished was the bootleg Sonic 4 for SNES, around 1999/2000.

What does your retro gaming collection look like?

Hoje ela é bem menor, tenho cerca de 10 ou 15 jogos de SNES e alguns de GB, GBA, DS e 3DS (se ele já for considerado retro). Jogo a maioria dos retros em emuladores, coletâneas oficiais e flashcarts.

Today my collection is much smaller; I have about 10 or 15 SNES games and some GB, GBA, DS, and 3DS games (if it's considered retro by now). I play most of the retro games on emulators, official collections, and flashcarts.

Do you have a favourite console?

SNES.

What games do you play regularly?

Super Mario RPG, Donkey Kong Country Trilogy, Smash Bros Ultimate, Pokémon.

What snacks/drinks keep you fuelled up for gaming?

Bolachas com café ou chips e refrigerante.

Cookies with coffee or chips and soda.

Do you have a prized gaming possession?

Provavelmente Chrono Trigger para Nintendo DS é meu jogo mais caro da coleção.

Chrono Trigger for Nintendo DS is probably my most expensive game in my collection.

Do you have any gaming unicorns left to obtain?

Meu sonho é ter todas as versões oficiais brasileiras de jogos da franquia Donkey Kong.

My dream is to own all the official Brazilian versions of the Donkey Kong franchise games.

Are you looking forward to any games in particular, regarding the recent N64 Recomp projects?

Não.

No.

You’re ordering pizza, you get three toppings, what’s your combo?

Calabresa, 4queijos e bacon.

Calabrese sausage, 4 cheeses, and bacon.

What’s your most expensive/thrifty retro purchase to date?

A mais cara foi The Legend of Zelda A Linkto the Past versão brasileira CIB e a mais econômica foram vários jogos de SNES que comprei de uma locadora em meu bairro que os vendeu por volta de 2012. Comprei dezenas de jogos por muito pouco dinheiro.

The most expensive was The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past (Brazilian version CIB), and the cheapest were several SNES games that I bought from a rental store in my neighborhood that sold them around 2012. I bought dozens of games for very little money.

Lastly: How many consoles do you own?

SNES, DS Lite, DSiXL, Wii, 3DS, WiiU, Xbox Series S, Nintendo Switch 2.

A Massive thank you to Nintendo Brasil Wiki for taking the time to be a part of ReCollect 64's Q&A chats!

Nintendo Brasil Wiki hosts an ongoing effort that is dedicated to preserving Nintendo's rich history with Brasil. Boxes, manuals, commercials, and everything else for a multitude of consoles that Nintendo officially released there are being painstakingly cataloged in one place. Their passion and thirst for completion is something I admire and wholeheartedly share.

In a fantastic demonstration of community spirit, we recently communicated and helped each other out regarding the Gradiente/Playtronic cover art for Quake that I required for ReCollect64 and the cover I recently aquired for Turok: Dinosaur Hunter that they required for the Nintendo Brasil Wiki.

I hope that we can help each other out again in the future, because I still need that elusive Tony Hawk's Pro Skater box art to complete the set on the ReCollect64 Collectors app, and I am always on the look out rare variants that they might not have images for yet.
