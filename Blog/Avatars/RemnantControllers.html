ReCollect64 Chats No. 30

Remnant Controllers! ~ Apr 30, 2025

Welcome to ReCollect64 chat! Let's kick-off this Q&A by describing your presence on social media for people:

My name is Paul David. I own and operate Remnant Controllers. I am active on Reddit, YouTube, and Bluesky where I share listings, restoration process photos, and short form N64 related videos.

When did you get into gaming, any formative recollections?

Gaming started at an early age for me. I got my start with Super Mario World on the SNES, but the console that blew the doors open was the N64. I will never forget the feeling of exploration and discovery.

What does your retro gaming collection look like?

Pretty N64 heavy, but I do also have a first-generation Wii! Shout out to my four personal N64 controllers: Ice blue, atomic purple, gold, and gray.

Do you have a favourite console?

N64 hands down!

What games do you play regularly?

Ocarina of Time gets a playthrough every year! I find myself replaying missions in Winback somewhat regularly. Currently running through Banjo-Tooie and it calls back to those memories of exploration and discovery I mentioned earlier.

What snacks/drinks keep you fuelled up for gaming?

I'm a water guy for the most part, but I have been known to crack open a glass bottled coke from time to time.

Do you have a prized gaming possession?

I modified an ice blue N64 controller with white buttons and I cannot ever see myself letting this one go. It's almost like part of my personality is in that controller now.

Do you have any gaming unicorns left to obtain?

It would be cool to say I have seen every N64 controller variant pass through the shop. Notably, a Millenium 2000 controller.

Are you looking forward to any games in particular, regarding the recent N64 Recomp projects?

Can't say I have my eye on anything at the moment.

You’re ordering pizza, you get three toppings, what’s your combo?

Ham, pepperoni, and onions.

What’s your most expensive/thrifty retro purchase to date?

That award goes out to my Ice Blue N64.

And Finally: How many consoles do you own?

A Charcoal Nintendo 64, Ice Blue Nintendo 64, Nintendo Wii, Nintendo Switch OLED and an Xbox Series S.

Thank you very much for your time and being part of ReCollect64 chats!

Based in Michigan, USA, Paul does some stunning work reinvigorating worn-out controllers, ensuring each one is thoroughly cleaned inside and out and respectfully reconditioned with rebuilt sticks.

Selling from just £18.78 for a basic grey OEM with a tight stick up to £94.10 for the crème de la crème featuring an 8BitDo wireless mod kit, an 8BitDo hall-effect stick, custom "blackout" buttons from Cartridge Blower and a BlueRetro Wireless Controller Adapter for N64, there are some absolutely killer finds on his site!
