ReCollect64 Chats No. 12

It's LuigiBlood! ~ Aug 13, 2024
@LuigiBlood

Welcome to ReCollect64 chat LuigiBlood! Lets kick-off this Q&A telling people how you would describe yourself on social media?

I do game preservation and some ROM hacks / translations and research about certain games, and some emulation work on obscure peripherals.

When did you get into gaming, any formative recollections?

I remember playing Super Mario All-Stars when the N64 was current gen (about to be superseded by the GameCube though).

What does your retro gaming collection look like?

I have a bunch of games for a bunch of consoles, but not necessarily filled with like tons of games, for example I just have like 5 N64 games. I have some noteworthy stuff, like an almost full collection of RPG Maker console games, but otherwise what I have are either from the collections of my family, or games that I decided to get (and for cheap). And then there's the Satellaview and 64DD which I got for free, for additional research and work.

Do you have a favourite console?

Super Nintendo, by a long shot. The Switch is probably second.

What games do you play regularly?

Splatoon 3 and F-Zero 99 as of now.

What snacks/drinks keep you fuelled up for gaming?

Probably chips lol

Do you have any prized gaming possessions?

Satellaview and 64DD. Given by the community who trusted me, and I always try to give back to them. I would never sell those.

Do you have any gaming unicorns left to obtain?

For games, Sound Fantasy on SNES. For a system, definitely the Game Processor SNES based hardware.

Are you looking forward to any particular games via N64 Recomp?

Mario Artist, once I get to it with some extra help to get started.

You’re ordering pizza, you get three toppings, what’s your combo?

I actually just get the most simple pizza. Cheese, Ham, Tomato. But I guess you could also say bologna but I'm definitely very boring.

What’s your most expensive/thrifty retro purchase to date?

RetroTink 5X Pro is what I spent the most for I think. My most expensive item is probably the 64DD.

How many games consoles do you own?

24 without counting variants. 35 with variants.

Variants definitely count! Thank you very much for your time and part of ReCollect64 chats!

LuigiBlood is a force of nature in the world of game preservation, and with 64DD games like the Mario Artist series getting the Recomp treatment, I cant wait to see whats next!
