Welcome to ReCollect64 chat MightyAndross64, lets begin this Q&A with a little about yourself on social media, do you have a mission?

My mission is to simply provide informative content from the Star Fox series. I usually cover a range of common & obscure materials that might prove valuable to the community & its fans. I also spend some time making art, listening to & creating music, or 3D modeling via Blender.

When did you get into gaming, any formative recollections?

My father got me into video games at a young age during the 1990s. My first 2 titles that I was introduced to was Super Mario World & Star Fox for the Snes. I was around 4 years old at the time I started to play video games.

What does your retro gaming collection look like?

Its not big, but mostly consists of games I was interested in. Half of it is game collectibles at this point hehe.

Do you have a favourite console?

That's a tough one lol. I'd probably go with the SNES Classic as my personal fav.

What games do you play regularly?

Super Mario 64, Star Fox 64, Super Mario World, Star Fox SNES, are just some I find myself going back to often.

What snacks/drinks keep you fuelled up for gaming?

A big bag of Bugles & some soda haha.

Do you have a prized gaming possession?

In terms of games, I don't think I do, but I would love to someday own a full set of the rare & expensive 1993 Takara Star Fox plushies lol.

Do you have any gaming unicorns left to obtain?

Hmm, there are a number of "unicorn" items I am searching for. The one that stands out the most to me would be the soundtrack for Wonder Project J2 for a decent price.
