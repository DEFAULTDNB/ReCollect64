Welcome to ReCollect64 chat Adam! Let's kick-off this Q&A by describing your presence on social media for people:

I'm a Youtuber! I pretty much strictly make GoldenEye 007 Content and I'm mainly known for my Tierlists and Funny Moments.

When did you get into gaming, any formative recollections?

I started gaming when I was 2 years old! My Mum was the one that got me into GoldenEye 007 as she was a massive fan of the movies and video game. But I did start my adventure gaming on the PlayStation and Sega Saturn.

What does your retro gaming collection look like?

I've been lucky to keep everything I had growing up so I have a decently sized "Retro Collection". Mostly from anything under the Sixth Generation of consoles.

Do you have a favourite console?

I would say the Nintendo 64 but the PlayStation 1 would be a close Second!

What games do you play regularly?

I don't really keep up with many modern releases I just stick to what I've always played such as. GoldenEye 007, Dead Space, Left 4 Dead, Mortal Kombat.

What snacks/drinks keep you fuelled up for gaming?

Can't beat a good Vegemite sandwich with a nice cold drink!

Do you have a prized gaming possession?

I would say all of my complete boxed Nintendo 64 games would be the main thing.

Do you have any gaming unicorns left to obtain?

I would say the GoldenEye 007 Beta.... Hopefully one day!

Are you looking forward to any games in particular, regarding the recent N64 Recomp projects?

I can't wait for the GoldenEye 007 Recomp! Another one was Mario Kart 64 that recently was released and I'm having a blast with it.

You’re ordering pizza, you get three toppings, what’s your combo?

I would say anything with MEAT!

What’s your most expensive/thrifty retro purchase to date?

I would say my Left 4 Dead 2 (2009) Gaming Heads Tank Statue! I found it for a really good price and it sells for about 20x the price I paid. That was a solid pickup for sure. Calling 2009 "Retro" is so weird!

Lastly: How many consoles do you own?

Alrighty! So I own Nintendo 64 (PAL), Nintendo 64 (JPN) , Sega Saturn (PAL), PlayStation 1 , PlayStation (ONE), PlayStation 2, PlayStation 2 (SLIM), PlayStation 3, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox Original, Xbox 360 (ARCADE), Xbox 360 (SLIM), Xbox One, Nintendo Gamecube, Nintendo Wii, Nintendo Switch, Nintendo Switch 2, Super Nintendo, Nintendo Gameboy, I think that is all of them.
