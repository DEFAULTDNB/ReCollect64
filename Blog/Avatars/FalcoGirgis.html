ReCollect64 Chats No. 37

Falco Girgis AKA Gyrovorbis ~ Aug 11, 2025
@Falco_Girgis

Welcome to ReCollect64 chat Falco! Let's kick-off this Q&A by describing your presence on social media for people:

I'm a computer engineer who is out there masquerading as one of the saviors of the Sega Dreamcast. lol.

When did you get into gaming, any formative recollections?

When I was in kindergarten. Long story, but we got an NES in a mail package one day, when I was 5, and it had Super Mario Bros with it, and I will never forget how playing that game felt at first...

What does your retro gaming collection look like?

MASSIVE. I own a pretty massive games and peripheral collection for: NES, SMS, SNES, Genesis, Sega CD/32x, PS1, Saturn, N64, Dreamcast, PS2, GC, Wii, Virtual Boy, GB, GBC, GBA, DS, 3DS... OH, and I have a GamesCom! I even liked that POS! I bet my wife can't wait till I die so she can sell it all...

Do you have a favourite console?

SEGA DREAMCAST!

What games do you play regularly?

Anything that comes out for Sega Dreamcast. lol. But when I was a little younger and hadn't had kids and had more time for gaming, I would beat a few games regularly... like Final Fantasy 12, Chrono Trigger, original NES Mega Mans, and Diddy Kong Racing.

What snacks/drinks keep you fuelled up for gaming?

Coffee or energy drinks depending on how unhealthy I'm feeling.

Do you have a prized gaming possession?

Yeah, if I'm completely honest with you, I value everything that I own for Dreamcast above everything else, because I can really justify owning and investing money into it as someone also developing for it...

But I was recently gifted an N64 Summercart, and I do follow that community quite closely and AT VERY LEAST wanted to support those cool dudes with it, so that does put probably N64 as a close second for the "prized" console stuff in my collection.

Do you have any gaming unicorns left to obtain?

OH MY GOD, YES. First of all, I gotta get a goddamn yellow VMU! It's worth as much as the Dreamcast is!!! Then basically everything else for DC. I want to own it all, since I'm already so deep... wouldn't mind something like an N64 HDD either, though. lol.

Are you looking forward to any games in particular, regarding the recent N64 Recomp projects?

Waverace 64.

You’re ordering pizza, you get three toppings, what’s your combo?

Ham, pineapple, bacon.

What’s your most expensive/thrifty retro purchase to date?

Probably, other than consoles, buying the Earthbound cart for SNES... Something like $250.

Lastly: How many consoles do you own?

1) Atari 2600, 2) Colecovision, 3) NES, 4) Sega Master System, 5) Sega Genesis, 6) Super Nintendo, 7) Sega CD, 8) Sega 32x, 9) Virtual Boy, 10) Sega Saturn, 11) Playstation 1, 12) Nintendo 64, 13) Sega Dreamcast, 14) Playstation 2, 15) Wii, 16) Playstation 3, 17) Xbox 360, 18) Xbox One, 19) Playstation 4, 20) Nintendo Switch, 21) GameCom, 22) Neo Geo Pocket, 23) Gameboy , 24) Gameboy Color, 25) Gameboy Advance, 26) Nintendo DS, 27) Nintendo 3DS.

Thank you very much for taking part in the ReCollect64 Q&A chats!

Falco is a Sega Dreamcast developer on a quest to correct the timeline of video gaming history. He is working on the modern, open-source indie DC SDK and library OS, KallistiOS, which is fuelling new developers projects, while he is also currently contributing to incredible porting projects that are blowing the Dreamcast scene wide open again.

With titles ranging from Grand Theft Auto III to Mario Kart 64, Star Fox 64, and potentially Wave Race 64 and beyond, the Dreamcast has a lot of incredible N64-related games on the way thanks to Falco and his enthusiastic team that includes jnmartin84 and SonicDcer!
