ReCollect64 Chats No. 10

N64Ever ~ July 22, 2024
@N64Ever

Welcome to ReCollect64 chat N64Ever, lets jump into this weeks Q&A by describing yourself on social media?

Just some #N64 lobbying. I have also my ugly website where I put a lot of content.

When did you get into gaming, any formative recollections?

Something like 5 years old, with the Amstrad CPC 6128. I was able to start the command with command lines!

What does your retro gaming collection look like?

I have four boxes full of N64 games. I've got around 40 original japanese games. I sold all my collection (every consoles, hundreds of games,...) 4 years ago.

Do you have a favourite console?

Obviously N64!

What games do you play regularly?

Many of them. I'm currently playing F1 24 and Unicorn overlord. For retrogaming, I like to replay a couple of N64 games like F Zero X or Jet Force Gemini. Crazy Taxi and Daytona USA are also games that I replay a lot.

What snacks/drinks keep you fuelled up for gaming?

Red wine.

Do you have a prized gaming possession?

Nope. I had in the past an AES Metal Slug.

Do you have any gaming unicorns left to obtain?

Not really, I'm not a game collector anymore.

Are you looking forward to any particular games via N64Recomp?

A lot! Like Zelda OOT, Wave Race 64, Diddy Kong Racing, snowboard Kids, Mace the Dark Age,... To have them 60 fps will be a benediction.

You’re ordering pizza, you get three toppings, what’s your combo?

Chicken, cheese, potato

What’s your most expensive/thrifty retro purchase to date?

My sports car. For videogames, I don't think I still own anything that's worth more than €60.

And finally, how many games consoles do you own?

PS5, Xbox 360, Xbox One, Wii U, PS Vita, 2x N64. I have also a Neo Geo arcade stick pro and the Mario / Zelda modern G&W. And a gaming PC! (11)

Thank you very much for your time and being part of ReCollect64 Q&A chats!
