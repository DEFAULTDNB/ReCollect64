Hello and welcome to another ReCollect64 Q&A session! Today we have AllN64Games, so lets begin this Q&A with a little about how you would describe yourself on social media:

Hi, I'm Dean and I'm trying to play every N64 game, sharing my thoughs with anyone willing to listen.

When did you get into gaming, any formative recollections?

When I was very young, I was given a Mega Drive and Sonic the Hedgehog, although I fully fell in love with gaming when I first tried Super Mario 64

What does your retro gaming collection look like?

Shambles, sadly, stuff lost over time and others sold in order to afford other things.

Do you have a favourite console?

It's really difficult to choose between the Nintendo 64 and the GameCube.

What games do you play regularly?

All sorts, it's part of why I started "play all X" projects (I've done Sonic, Star Trek and James Bond games), to play games I would usually overlook.

Do you have a prized gaming possession?

A custom cover/case for Burnout Paradise with a thank you message from the developers for helping them out.

Do you have any gaming unicorns left to obtain?

my unicorn would be more a lost game or prototype: Rogue Squadron Wii, Sabreman Stampede and Sonic's Timed Train.

Are you looking forward to any games via N64 Recomp?

Better camera control for the likes of Rocket: Robot on Wheels and Space Station Silicon Valley would be great

You’re ordering pizza, you get three toppings, what’s your combo?

Bacon, tuna and mushrooms.

How many games consoles do you own?

A Mega Drive, N64, GameCube, GBA, Wii, DS, Xbox 360, PS3, 3DS,PS4, Wii U, Switch and Xbox Series S (13).

What’s your most expensive/thrifty retro purchase to date?

Banjo-Kazooie transformation statues are my most expensive recent gaming items, made by a fan.

What snacks/drinks keep you fuelled up for gaming?

A nice hot cup of tea.
