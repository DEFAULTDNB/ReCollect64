ReCollect64 Chats No. 11

It's Kurko Mods! ~ Jul 27, 2024
@Kurko Mods

Welcome to ReCollect 64! Lets kick-off this Q&A telling people how you would describe yourself on social media?

Right now my social media (Twitter, Instagram, threads & TikTok) are focused on entertaining people with retro content from the 90s/Early's 00s. Every day I make 2 different posts, one is some old commercial or beta video, and the other is a comparison between two games. I love reading people's opinions on each game, and why they prefer one or the other. On the other hand, every month I restore 25 retro arts, which I use to make these "one or another" publications, and I also publish 25 Wallpapers and 25 phone backgrounds I make using these restored retro arts, but that is exclusive for the people who support me on Patreon and Youtube membership. And finally, I also continue working on my own Banjo Kazooie projects and try to record videos about other people's projects for YouTube.

When did you get into gaming, any formative recollections?

I started playing when I was 5 years old, when my parents bought one of those NES clones in 1993. For several years I only had one cartridge that included a handful of games like Pacman, Contra, Super Mario Bros, Mappy, Arkanoid etc. I remember that about 1995 they bought SMB3, and that was all until Christmas 1999 when I was finally able to buy N64 with the money I had saved for a long time.I remember drawing Super Mario comics, and imagining designing Banjo Kazooie levels by drawing them on a sheet of paper. I also remember drawing a lot of Zelda, and designing a board game that I never finished. I guess all of that was a bit of a seed for wanting to later dedicate myself to developing video games.

What does your retro gaming collection look like?

I'm not a big retro collector, possibly the console I have the most games on is the N64. I remember buying a lot of second-hand games in the early 00s, before the whole retro boom sent prices skyrocketing.

Do you have a favourite console?

N64, without a doubt. It is the console that I have the most love for everything it meant to me, and for what it cost me to get it. However, I think that right now Nintendo Switch is the best console that has ever existed

What games do you play regularly?

I don't have much time to play, so I don't usually replay many games. However, from time to time I always play Zelda Ocarina of Time.

Do you have a prized gaming possession?

If you mean prized possession for me, the N64. I think it's the only console I have that I wouldn't sell for anything in the world. If you mean prized with real value, maybe Conker, since I have it with a box in good condition, or the collector's version of Skyward Sword with the golden WiiMote

Do you have any gaming unicorns left to obtain?

As I already told you, I'm not a big game collector. However, I would like to have some Japanese N64 games with box included. Super Mario 64, the two Zelda and the two Banjo to give a few examples.

Are you looking forward to any games via N64 Recomp?

I was trying out Majora just the other day, and I quite liked the experience, so I guess when Ocarina is available it will be time to play it again lol

You’re ordering pizza, you get three toppings, what’s your combo?

Meat, cheese and barbecue sauce. I'm pretty simple haha

What’s your most expensive/thrifty gaming purchase to date?

Currently, it's probably my PC, but it's not a high-end PC or anything

What snacks/drinks keep you fuelled up for gaming?

As for international snacks, I have always been a big fan of Doritos. And now there are some snacks that are made here in Spain called Jumpers that are really delicious!

Finally: How many games consoles do you own?

I have the NES clone, two N64s, Wii, Ps2, Ps3, Xbox360, WiiU, Nintendo Switch, NDS and N3DS. So 10, 11 counting the second N64. I don't have a Gamecube because I had to sell it years ago due to lack of money.
