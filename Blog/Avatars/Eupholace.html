ReCollect64 Chats No. 25

Eupholace! ~ Feb 8, 2025

Welcome to ReCollect64 chat Eupholace! Let's kick-off this Q&A by describing your presence on social media for people:

Hey! I’m William AKA Eupholace, and I mainly enjoy posting about my gamedev projects and whatever other random hobbies I’m into at the moment. I also work for Moon Studios as a level designer, so social media is a nice opportunity for me to show my work there as well!

When did you get into gaming, any formative recollections?

My earliest gaming memories are probably from when I was about 4 or 5 - I have a lot of older siblings, so I usually just played whatever they owned. That meant I had a bit more retro game exposure than someone my age might normally have. The games I remember most from that time would be Super Smash Bros. Melee and Super Mario Sunshine.

What does your retro gaming collection look like?

Unfortunately, I sold what few retro games I owned when the Nintendo Switch was announced so I could save up for it. I was barely too young to have a job back then, so I needed to scrounge up cash wherever I could - the console just looked too exciting to miss! I do remember that I used to personally own Super Mario Land 2 and F-Zero X, but again, most of the retro games I played were ones my siblings owned.

Do you have a favourite console?

Probably my Switch, it was the first big console I personally owned and it has many of my favorite games. The Wii was also particularly special to me.

What games do you play regularly?

It’s a bit embarrassing, but I’m a bit of an avid Fortnite player, haha. That’s sort of my comfort game to play with friends. For more traditional games, I typically enjoy going back to Terraria, Spelunky 2, and Mario Odyssey. I’m not usually one to replay games though, if I do I prefer to replay when it’s been long enough that I’ve forgotten much about it.

What snacks/drinks keep you fuelled up for gaming?

Nothing! I don’t really like snacking while gaming, and I’m mostly just a water drinker. Being both a professional and hobbyist gamedev means I’m sometimes sitting for upwards of 10-12 hours a day, so I’ve started trying to prioritize my health more. I do enjoy my daily cup of coffee in the morning though!

Do you have a prized gaming possession?

I wouldn’t say so, I’m not exactly a collector - but the most valuable one I have at least is probably my physical copy of Super Mario 3D All-Stars, I’m sure that thing sells for a few hundred bucks on eBay these days.

Do you have any gaming unicorns left to obtain?

There’s nothing I’m really looking for, but I feel like as a gamedev, I’d really love to have some original physical game design document or something. Although, I imagine those aren’t exactly lying around on sale in many places!

Are you looking forward to any games in particular, regarding the recent N64 Recomp projects?

Not exactly, I’m not a modder. I would have said Banjo-Kazooie if any, but that’s been done! So next would be Banjo-Tooie I suppose.

You’re ordering pizza, you get three toppings, what’s your combo?

Hmm, that’s tough. Usually sausage is enough, but if I had to pick two more, maybe mushrooms and pepperoni.

What’s your most expensive/thrifty retro purchase to date?

Don’t think I’ve made any notable ones! Although I’d probably like to eventually, I’ve been to a few retro games stores recently and I think that ignited the desire to get something.

And Finally: How many consoles do you own?

SNES Classic, Ps4 Pro, Ps5 (disc version), Nintendo Switch (4)

Thank you very much for your time and being part of ReCollect64 chats!

Eupholace's entry within a game developing environment such as Moon Studios is an incredibly aspirational story of how crafting in a game can lead to professional job offers!

Most recently, he has been working with Piece of Craft on creating some stunning Banjo-Kazooie themed creations in Dreams for Playstation 4. You'd better believe that I am intently keeping tabs on this project because it's wonderfully nostalgic!
