ReCollect64 Chats No. 43

Rovertronic ~ Nov 13, 2025

Welcome to ReCollect64 chat Rovert! Let's kick-off this Q&A by describing a little about yourself.

Rovertronic, SM64 ROM hacker, VR Enthusiast, Software Dev.

When did you get into gaming, any formative recollections?

When I was 4 years old, my older brother had me play mario 64 on pj64 on our old family PC.

What does your retro gaming collection look like?

Pretty sparse, I don't really collect much stuff.

Do you have a favourite console?

Nintendo 64.

What games do you play regularly?

Mostly physics based VR games or simulation games like factorio and teardown.

What snacks/drinks keep you fuelled up for gaming?

TBH I mostly just drink zevia soda these days, sometimes i buy random snacks from the grocery store for fun.

Do you have a prized gaming possession?

Not really, not a collector.

Do you have any gaming unicorns left to obtain?

(See above)

Are you looking forward to any games in particular, regarding the recent N64 Recomp projects?

Maybe I'll make a Mario in the Multiverse recomp someday, so that it can be played with Archipelago.

You’re ordering pizza, you get three toppings, what’s your combo?

Pepperoni, Sausage, Bacon.

What’s your most expensive/thrifty retro purchase to date?

I haven't bought too much retro stuff since I'm mostly spend time writing software, so my real N64 for console testing.

Lastly: How many consoles do you own?

N64, Switch 1, (PC?) (I used to have a lot more as a kid but I've given them away since).

Big thanks to Rovertronic for being part of ReCollect64's Q&A chats!
