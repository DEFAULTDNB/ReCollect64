ReCollect64 Chats No. 18

Kode-Z ~ Nov 4, 2024
@Kode-Z

Welcome to ReCollect64 chat Kode-Z, aka AlDavisMusic, Lets kick-off this Q&A telling people how you would describe your presence on social media?

I’m a remixer/producer from London, UK. I use social media for gaming related posts, and to share my music projects (remixes, custom game tracks. I used to create Action Replay codes for GoldenEye and Perfect Dark).

When did you get into gaming, any formative recollections?

My dad bought me and my brother a NES in the tail end of the 80s, followed by a GameBoy in the mid 90s.

What does your retro gaming collection look like?

At the moment, I have mainly N64 related stuff - boxed games, controllers & peripherals.

Do you have a favourite console?

The Xbox 360. When it was released, it just ticked the right boxes for me as a gamer. The XBLA section was a great way to play indie games and retro classics.

What games do you play regularly?

Recently, I’ve been replaying the Resident Evil games. I’ve also been replaying “The World Is Not Enough” on the N64!

What snacks/drinks keep you fuelled up for gaming?

Pringles and a chilled coffee!

Do you have a prized gaming possession?

My boxed copies of the N64 Zelda games.

Do you have any gaming unicorns left to obtain?

Probably a Japanese copy of Perfect Dark.

Are you looking forward to any games in particular, regarding the recent N64 Recomp projects?

Definitely GoldenEye! As a former Action Replay code creator of the game, it’ll be interesting to see what people implement into the game down the line!

You’re ordering pizza, you get three toppings, what’s your combo?

Chicken, minced beef and jalapeño peppers!

What’s your most expensive/thrifty retro purchase to date?

I have a handful of mint condition N64 boxed games.

And Finally: How many consoles do you own?

7.

Thank you very much for your time and being part of ReCollect64 chats!

Kode-Z produces some of the best custom GoldenEye 007 and Perfect Dark tracks I have ever heard, blending modern ideas with the original soundfont to craft some stunningly beautiful pieces that are sensitive to how the original composers, such as Grant Kirkhope and Graeme Norgate, would have created them!

They also remix other iconic retro games and TV show/movie soundtracks, such as Doom and Xmen '97 in the style of Rare's classic shooters, which cranks the nostalgia up to another level!
