Rules & Regulations

This page holds all International Track and Field/Summer Games/Ganbare! Nippon! Olympic 2000 records in distinct tables.

This is the current set of rules to keep things fair:

  1. Flashcarts are allowed, but no ROM hacks, patches, or cheats may be used other than the Konami code.

  2. Emulator records may not be accepted due to accuracy, input lag, and timings. Emulated times were slower than console when tested.

  3. Proof can be submitted in photo form, from an uploaded video of your replay, or a confirmed scores site such as cyberscore.me.uk.

    (NOTE: Images of your proofs will be stored on this site for ease of access and to prevent removal elsewhere)

  4. The following information must accompany your submission: your username, country of residence, team used, time/distance, date achieved, & game + console region.

  5. Please specify the game+console region using these examples: "PAL GAME/CONSOLE" or "USA GAME/JPN CONSOLE".

  6. You can SUBMIT RECORDS HERE or use the link below.

  7. Any altered or suspicious submissions will be removed, and players will be banned from participating.

  8. Anyone can enter regardless of skill level, it's all in the spirit of community-building fun.

  9. The competition has no end date, and you may enter as many times, and as frequently, as you like.

Note: To unlock all events, enter the Konami code at the "Press Start" screen: ⬆️⬆️⬇️⬇️⬅️➡️⬅️➡️🅱️🅰️.
