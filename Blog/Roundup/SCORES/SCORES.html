HALL OF FAME

Collated Critiques

Here at ReCollect64, I try to remain objective. Whether something is the greatest thing since sliced bread or stinks like a turd on fire, I will always score it accordingly. I try to give everything the time and any benefit of the doubt it may deserve, but ultimately it all comes from me, direct from my unbiased, unpaid, personal opinions.

In March of 2025, I will have been reviewing games and tech for six full years, so I know from experience that reviews are subjective. If a game or accessory resonates with you, you give it a glowing score; if you don't get the point, or you're having a hard time with it, you tend to mark it down as the thing being reviewed hasn't made quite such a positive impact with you.

At the end of the day, these are purely my thoughts, so while I actively encourage sharing and commenting, please remember that my opinion might not align with yours, and that's ok; we can still be friends.

The sole purpose of this page is to collate all reviews on this site into an easily readable tabular format, with a link to each review and a "X/10" overview of how they scored!

I've sorted them in the order of first down to worst, and hope that you enjoy them and feel compelled to share some of these with your friends and peers online too.
